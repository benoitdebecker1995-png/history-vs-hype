## Mechanism Forensics: Key Discovery

**This technique is the most valuable finding from Audit #2.**

PolyMatter doesn't just say "Singapore is authoritarian." He shows the EXACT MECHANISMS:
- 11-day campaign periods
- Gerrymandering (19,000 vs 38,000 voter districts)
- Lawsuit tactics against opposition
- "See if you can find what they had in common" (dissolved districts)

**Application to History vs Hype:**

Instead of:
> "France exploited Haiti through debt."

Use Mechanism Forensics:
> "France's extraction worked like this:
>
> First, the 150 million franc demand—payable in five annual installments of 30 million.
> Haiti couldn't pay, so they borrowed from French banks at 6% interest.
>
> It gets far worse.
>
> The 1825 ordinance also required Haiti to halve tariffs on French goods—
> cutting the government's main revenue source while increasing payments.
>
> In other words, France designed a system where Haiti paid to be drained."

## Retention Pattern Analysis

### RealLifeLore Structure (Europe Disputes)
- **0:00-0:30:** Count hook + severity structure announcement
- **0:30-2:00:** "Mild" category (low-stakes examples)
- **2:00-5:00:** "Moderate" category
- **5:00-end:** "Serious" category (high-stakes examples)

**Pattern:** Builds from low to high stakes. Viewers stay to reach the "serious" ones.

### PolyMatter Structure (Singapore)
- **0:00-1:30:** Double Assumption Subversion (hooks by confusing twice)
- **1:30-2:30:** Meta-Reflection + Stakes
- **2:30-6:00:** Historical context (how Singapore got here)
- **6:00-10:00:** Mechanism Forensics (HOW the system works)
- **10:00-end:** Implications + viewer challenge

**Pattern:** Constant reframes. Every time viewer thinks they understand, complexity added.

## Key Insight: The PolyMatter Formula

PolyMatter's Singapore video achieves what History vs Hype should aim for:

1. **Intellectual rigor** - Not dumbed down, assumes smart audience
2. **Mechanism focus** - Shows HOW, not just WHAT
3. **No easy answers** - "The answer reveals as much about your views"
4. **Stacked complexity** - Each section adds nuance, never simplifies

**The difference:** PolyMatter uses news sources. History vs Hype uses academic sources with page numbers.

This is the unfair advantage. PolyMatter's mechanism forensics + History vs Hype's source transparency = channel differentiation.
