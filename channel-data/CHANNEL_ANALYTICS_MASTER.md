## 🎯 SUCCESSFUL FORMULA (Proven)

### **Structure:**
1. **Hook (0-15 sec):** Modern political figure using historical myth
2. **Payoff (0:45):** Primary document contradicting myth
3. **Pattern (2-10 min):** 3 interconnected claims with evidence
4. **Modern Stakes (final 2 min):** Why accurate history matters NOW
5. **CTA:** Sources in description, subscribe for more

### **Retention Optimized:**
- First payoff at 0:45 (prevents 1:11 drop)
- Micro-hooks every 2 minutes
- Visual variety every 30-45 seconds
- "But it gets worse" transitions

### **Visual Strategy:**
- 60-70% talking head (you are the authority)
- 15-20% maps (evidence)
- 10-15% primary documents (smoking guns)
- 5% modern footage (news hooks)
