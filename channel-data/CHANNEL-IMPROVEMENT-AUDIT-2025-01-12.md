## Key Insight: The "Mechanism > Politics" Rule

**VidIQ validated pattern:** Your audience subscribes for "intellectual competence" - proving you understand SYSTEMS, not just narratives.

From competitor analysis:
- **Wendover:** "Hawaii's Logistics Problem" = systems explanation
- **Historia Civilis:** Explains mechanisms (army logistics, political structure)
- **Fall of Civilizations:** Explains collapse mechanisms, not just events

**Apply to History vs Hype:**
- "How they deleted a country" (mechanism) > "Why they split" (politics)
- Show the LOGISTICS of colonial extraction, not just the injustice
- Explain the MECHANISM of treaty enforcement, not just the politics
