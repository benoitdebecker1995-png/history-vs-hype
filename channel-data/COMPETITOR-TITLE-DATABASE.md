### Smaller Channels to Watch (Under 500K)

| Channel | Size | Why |
|---------|------|-----|
| **Khanubis** | ~300K | Historical myths, nationalism, source-aware essays |

### Channels to AVOID Copying

| Channel | Why Not |
|---------|---------|
| **Kurzgesagt** | Heavy abstraction, cutesy animation — your edge is "receipts" |
| **Oversimplified** | Comedy-first, meme-driven — clashes with legal-document focus |
| **AlternateHistoryHub** | Speculative scenarios — you're anchored to real treaties/archives |
| **Mr. Beat** | Classroom feel, soft explanations — you're hard-nosed myth-busting |
| **Vox** | Fast-cut news-magazine, light on raw documents |

### What to STEAL vs AVOID from Each Reference

| Channel | STEAL | AVOID |
|---------|-------|-------|
| **Kraut** | Thesis → Context → Evidence Cases → Rebuttal → Implications structure | Multi-hour runtimes, sprawling digressions |
| **Knowing Better** | Personal stakes + anecdotes in first 20-30 seconds | Heavy editorial snark, "I'm right, they're dumb" posture |
| **Shaun** | Forensic claim-by-claim debunking with on-screen citations | Creator drama, reaction-video framing |
| **Fall of Civilizations** | Atmosphere: music, ambient sound, quotes read over visuals (10-20 sec bursts) | 3-5 hour runtimes, ultra-slow build-ups |
| **RealLifeLore** | Map-heavy thumbnails, "impossible situation" titles | Surface-level sourcing, sweeping simplifications |
| **Wendover** | Narrative spine: one question → 3-4 logical steps | General-audience smoothing of controversy |

### The History vs Hype Formula (Synthesized)

1. **Open like Knowing Better** (20-30s personal stake)
2. **Structure like Kraut** (Thesis → Context → Evidence → Rebuttal → Implications)
3. **Argue like Shaun** (claim → evidence on screen)
4. **Package like RealLifeLore** (maps, "impossible border" framing)
5. **Sprinkle Fall of Civilizations mood** (10-15 seconds per section)
