## TOPIC EVALUATION CHECKLIST

Before committing to a topic, verify:

### Channel DNA Alignment
- [ ] Is the historical document/event the MAIN content (not just background)?
- [ ] Will this topic matter in 10 years regardless of who's in power?
- [ ] Can I show primary documents on screen?
- [ ] Is there a clear modern relevance hook?

### Outlier Pattern Match
- [ ] Does it fit "Map They Ignored" pattern? (comparison available)
- [ ] Does it fit "Legal Fiction" pattern? (claim vs reality gap)
- [ ] Does it fit "Specific Site" pattern? (microcosm of larger issue)
- [ ] Does it fit "Deadline Approaching" pattern? (natural urgency)

### Documentary Feasibility
- [ ] Are primary sources accessible (archives, Wikisource, etc.)?
- [ ] Can I get real academic quotes with page numbers?
- [ ] Is there enough for 10-25 minutes of content?
- [ ] Do I have or can I create map/document B-roll?

### Red Flags (Skip or Reframe)
- [ ] ❌ News-first framing required
- [ ] ❌ Requires current politician as main subject
- [ ] ❌ No primary documents available
- [ ] ❌ Would need clickbait language to hook
