**Don't panic.** This is why we fact-check.

**Fix:**

3. MEDIUM errors can wait for next video

**Fix:**

- I'll calculate actual years

- Format: Quote + Attribution + Date
- Still credible, easier to create

## VOICE & TONE ISSUES

### "Script sounds too formal"

**Red flags I check:**
- ❌ "Here's the thing" (overused)
- ❌ "Now - " at every transition
- ❌ "You might be wondering..."
- ❌ Long buildup before evidence

**Your actual patterns:**
- ✅ Short sentences: "They won."
- ✅ Direct transitions: "So when does..."
- ✅ Minimal buildup: "Exact quote:" then quote
- ✅ Fragments for emphasis: "1452. Pope Nicholas V."

**Fix:** Tell me specific lines that sound wrong

## GETTING UNSTUCK

**If you're stuck for more than 30 minutes:**

1. Close all the guides
2. Ask me ONE specific question
3. I'll tell you exactly what to do next

**Example good questions:**
- "Which source should I use for [claim]?"
- "Is this line too formal: [quote line]?"
- "Should I cut this section or keep it?"

**Example bad questions:**
- "What should I do?" (too vague)
- "Is this good?" (need specific concern)
- "Help?" (tell me what's wrong)
