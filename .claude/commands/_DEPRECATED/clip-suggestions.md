I'll analyze your script and identify the best moments to extract as standalone clips for YouTube Shorts, TikTok, or social media.

## What Makes a Good Clip

### Clip Criteria (All Must Apply)
1. **Self-contained** - Makes sense without full video context
2. **Hook in first 2 seconds** - Grabs attention immediately
3. **Single clear point** - One fact, one reveal, one argument
4. **30-60 seconds** - Sweet spot for shorts
5. **Strong ending** - Ends on impact, not mid-thought

### Best Clip Types for History vs Hype

| Type | Example | Why It Works |
|------|---------|--------------|
| **Smoking Gun Reveal** | "Here's what the document actually says..." | Evidence on screen = shareable |
| **Myth Bust Moment** | "You've heard X. It's wrong. Here's why." | Controversy drives engagement |
| **Shocking Statistic** | "Not 300,000. The Nazi records show 1.27 million." | Numbers stick in memory |
| **Quote Takedown** | "[Person] claimed X. The primary source says Y." | Debate format works on social |
| **Map Comparison** | "Look at 1916. Now look at today. They don't match." | Visual proof is compelling |
