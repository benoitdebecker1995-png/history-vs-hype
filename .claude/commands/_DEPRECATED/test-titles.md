I'll generate title variants for your video that balance documentary authority with YouTube click-through optimization.

## Channel Title Standards (Non-Negotiable)

**DO:**
- 60-70 characters (mobile-friendly)
- Factually accurate
- Documentary tone
- Explicit about sensitive topics
- Include the controversy/hook

**DON'T:**
- Clickbait ("You won't BELIEVE...")
- ALL CAPS words
- Excessive punctuation (!!!)
- Misleading promises
- Hide sensitive subjects

## What I Need

1. **Topic:** What's the video about?
2. **Hook:** What's the modern relevance / controversy?
3. **Key claim:** What are you fact-checking or debunking?
4. **Target figure (if any):** Politician, influencer, or movement being addressed?

## What You'll Get

**10 title variants across 4 categories:**

### Category 1: Fact-Check Frame (3 titles)
Format: "Fact-Checking [Person/Claim]: [What Evidence Shows]"
- Best for: Political figures, viral claims
- Example: "Fact-Checking Nick Fuentes: What the Nazi Documents Actually Say"

### Category 2: Myth-Bust Frame (3 titles)
Format: "The [Myth] Myth: [Reality]" or "[Common Belief] Is Wrong. Here's Why."
- Best for: Historical misconceptions
- Example: "The Dark Ages Myth: Medieval Europe Wasn't Backward"

### Category 3: Documentary Frame (2 titles)
Format: "[Topic]: [Specific Aspect] Explained" or "Why [X] Actually [Y]"
- Best for: Complex historical topics
- Example: "Sykes-Picot: Why This Map Didn't Create the Middle East"

### Category 4: Question/Curiosity Frame (2 titles)
Format: "Did [X] Really [Y]?" or "What Really Happened at [Event]?"
- Best for: SEO, search discovery
- Example: "Did the Crusades Start as Self-Defense?"
