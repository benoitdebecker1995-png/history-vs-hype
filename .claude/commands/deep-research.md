I'll do a deep-dive on your topic using only freely available sources - before you invest in expensive books.

## What You'll Get

A comprehensive research report including:
- Smoking gun evidence (5-10 primary sources with quotes)
- Pattern analysis (3+ interconnected incidents)
- Academic consensus summary
- Counter-arguments to address
- Modern relevance examples
- Video structure outline
- Viral potential assessment
- Clear YES/MAYBE/NO verdict on viability

## What I'll Research

**Free sources only:**
- Wikipedia (overview + bibliography mining)
- Government archives (treaties, documents, official records)
- Google Scholar (previews, free PDFs)
- News articles
- Primary source databases
- Academic repositories

**Deep analysis:**
- Overview & context (30 min equivalent)
- Primary source hunting (45 min equivalent)
- Academic consensus (30 min equivalent)
- Counter-argument research (20 min equivalent)
- Modern connection research (15 min equivalent)

## What I Need From You

1. **Topic:** What myth/claim are we debunking?
2. **Modern hook:** What recent event makes this timely?
3. **Initial hypothesis:** What do you think the evidence will show?
