# Sykes-Picot Video - Shot-by-Shot Editing Guide

**Video Runtime:** 11:09 (01:00:00 - 01:11:09)
**Format:** Hybrid Talking Head (60-70% on camera, 30-40% B-roll evidence)
**Golden Rule:** Stay on camera when making arguments. Cut to B-roll when showing evidence.

---

## EDITING PHILOSOPHY

**You Stay On Camera When:**
- Making arguments and interpretations
- Explaining causation and connecting events
- Building narrative tension
- Asking rhetorical questions
- Emphasizing emotional points

**Cut to B-Roll When:**
- Showing maps, documents, primary sources
- Presenting visual evidence that proves your point
- Displaying dates, statistics, quotes
- Need pattern interrupt (every 2-3 min)

**If the B-roll doesn't make your argument stronger, stay on camera.**
