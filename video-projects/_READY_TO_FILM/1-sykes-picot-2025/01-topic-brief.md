## The Myth to Debunk

**Primary Myth:** "Sykes-Picot created the artificial borders that cause all Middle East conflicts today"

**Secondary Myth:** "The 1916 Sykes-Picot map was never actually used"

---

## The Nuanced Truth

Sykes-Picot was a significant blueprint for post-Ottoman territorial divisions that influenced the League of Nations mandate system, but it was modified by subsequent events (Arab Revolt, Balfour Declaration, local negotiations). The agreement didn't create "artificial borders" - it was one factor among many in a complex colonial partitioning process.
