## KEY DIFFERENCES FROM ORIGINAL

> "So, December 8th, 2024. Israel invades Syria, right? And they seize like several hundred square miles of Syrian territory."

- ✅ "right?" - Engages listener

- ✅ "And they" - Connects thoughts naturally
- ✅ Flows like speaking, not reading headlines
