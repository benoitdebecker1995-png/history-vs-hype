# Validated Performance Patterns - Statistical Analysis

**Analysis Date:** 2025-01-19
**Dataset:** 165 total videos (53 long-form ≥90s, 112 shorts <90s)
**Analysis Focus:** Long-form videos only (shorts excluded due to different algorithm dynamics)

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**Key Finding:** Modern news hooks are the single strongest predictor of performance, increasing average views by 89% compared to purely historical content.

**Top Patterns:**
1. **Modern Hook Effect:** Videos with 2023-2025 news hooks average 347 views vs 184 views for historical content (+89%)
2. **Territorial Disputes Dominate:** This topic type has the highest average views (312) and most Tier 1 performers
3. **Political Fact-Checks:** Highest CTR (5.8% avg) and subscriber conversion (2.4 subs/video)
4. **Sweet Spot Length:** 8-12 minute videos outperform shorter and longer content
5. **Retention Mystery:** High retention doesn't guarantee high views - audience targeting matters more

## 6. RETENTION MYSTERY: HIGH RETENTION ≠ HIGH VIEWS

### Highest Retention Videos (Long-form):

1. Did Vikings Have Female Commanders: 18.24% (626 views)
2. How a 1947 Drawing Changed World: 13.62% (90 views)
3. Truth About Taiwan-China: 12.43% (630 views - short at 44s)
4. KGB Weaponized Palestinians: 11.72% (148 views)
5. Why Belize Disappears Guatemala Maps: 12.88% (838 views - short at 41s)

### Pattern Analysis:

**High Retention + High Views (Rare):**
- Did Vikings Have Female Commanders: 18.24% retention, 626 views
- Venezuela-Guyana: 9.57% retention, 1,905 views

**High Retention + Low Views (Common):**
- How a 1947 Drawing: 13.62% retention, 90 views
- KGB Palestinians: 11.72% retention, 148 views
- Peru Poorest Regions: 8.85% retention, 26 views
- Why Kosovo True Story: 8.30% retention, 27 views

### Why High Retention Doesn't Guarantee Views:

1. **Algorithm Discovery:** Retention helps promotion AFTER initial clicks, but doesn't drive initial impressions
2. **Thumbnail/Title Failure:** Great content with poor packaging gets low CTR
3. **Niche Topics:** Highly engaged micro-audiences (KGB history, Kashmir treaties) don't scale
4. **Upload Timing:** Some high-retention videos are very recent and need time

### Actionable Insight:

**Focus order should be:**
1. **Topic Selection** (modern hook > pure history)
2. **Title/Thumbnail CTR** (get the click first)
3. **Content Quality/Retention** (keep them watching)

High retention on niche topics = wasted effort. Better to have 8% retention on a clickable topic than 15% retention on something nobody clicks.

## 9. VALIDATED SUCCESS FORMULA

### Based on Statistical Analysis:

**TIER 1 Video (1000+ views) Recipe:**
1. **Topic:** Territorial dispute with oil/resource angle OR political fact-check of named figure
2. **Modern Hook:** Explicit 2023-2025 news connection in title
3. **Length:** 8-10 minutes (sweet spot for algorithm + retention)
4. **Title Formula:** "[Current Event/Figure] + [Shocking Claim] + [Evidence Word]"
   - Examples: "JD Vance Claims..." (936 views), "Venezuela vs Guyana: The Oil War" (1,905 views)
5. **CTR Target:** Aim for 4%+ (requires strong thumbnail + title combo)
6. **Retention Target:** 8%+ (achievable with good pacing)

**TIER 2 Video (500-999 views) Recipe:**
1. **Topic:** Ongoing territorial dispute without immediate news hook OR historical myth with modern relevance
2. **Modern Connection:** Indirect relevance to current events (Turkey-Greece tensions, Israel-Palestine context)
3. **Length:** 9-12 minutes
4. **Title Formula:** "Why [Country] + [Action Verb] + [Geographic Feature]"
   - Example: "Why Turkey and Greece Can't Agree on These Islands" (901 views)
5. **CTR Target:** 3%+
6. **Retention Target:** 10%+ (compensate for weaker hook with better content)

**TIER 3+ Video (200-499 views) Baseline:**
1. **Topic:** Pure historical analysis OR colonial legacy
2. **Modern Connection:** Weak or absent
3. **Length:** Any (length matters less when discovery is limited)
4. **Title Formula:** Generic question format
5. **CTR Target:** 2-3%
6. **Retention Target:** 7-9%

## 10. ANTI-PATTERNS: WHAT DEFINITELY DOESN'T WORK

### Based on Underperforming Videos:

**Topic Anti-Patterns:**
1. **CFA Franc/France-Africa Content:** Averages 82 views despite moral importance
2. **Historical Treaty Analysis:** Videos about 1846, 1859, 1899 treaties average 188 views
3. **Kosovo Content:** 7 videos, averaging 132 views - saturated topic or poor execution
4. **Stalin Historical Analysis:** 3 videos, averaging 136 views - doesn't break through

**Title Anti-Patterns:**
1. **Year-First Titles:** "1899 Fraud," "1947 Drawing" - CTR below 2%
2. **Generic Questions:** "What Really Happened in Kosovo?" - CTR 0.67%
3. **Academic Framing:** "Why Kosovo's Story Got Twisted?" - CTR 0.45%
4. **Vague "Secrets":** "Turkey's Darkest Secret" - CTR 2.09% (should be higher)

**Length Anti-Patterns:**
1. **12+ minutes without mega-hook:** Averages 89 views
2. **Under 3 minutes on deep topics:** Too short to build authority

**Execution Anti-Patterns:**
1. **High retention + no views:** Content is good but packaging failed
2. **High impressions + low CTR:** Algorithm tried to help but title/thumbnail failed
3. **Low impressions + low CTR:** Complete algorithm miss - wrong topic or bad timing
