## WHAT'S WORKING (Validated Patterns)

### 1. Territorial Dispute Formula
- **Pattern:** "[Colonial Decision] is still [causing modern stakes] today"
- **Examples:** Belize (28K), Essequibo (2K), Turkey-Greece (914)
- **Elements:** Maps, treaty documents, ICJ cases, modern consequences

### 2. Length Sweet Spot
- **Optimal:** 9-12 minutes (35%+ retention achievable)
- **Evidence:** Top 5 long-form all in this range
- **Shorts (<90s):** Higher views but no subscriber conversion

### 3. Visual Evidence Strategy
- **Map thumbnails > Face thumbnails** (26x better performance)
- **Documents on screen** = higher retention
- **VidIQ scores don't predict documentary performance**

### 4. Title Formulas That Work
- "The [X] That Might [Consequence]" - Belize (28K)
- "[Country] vs [Country]: The [Stakes]" - Essequibo (2K)
- "[Person] Claims [X]. Here's the Evidence." - JD Vance (1.1K)

### 5. News Hook Timing
- ICJ rulings, treaty anniversaries, current conflicts
- Belize published during ICJ news cycle = algorithm boost

## WHAT'S NOT WORKING

### 1. Shorts Strategy
- High views but ~0 subscriber conversion
- Watch time negligible (1-3 hrs vs 100+ for long-form)
- Not building channel authority

### 2. Academic Titles
- "What 3 ICJ Cases Show" (4.9K) vs "Might Disappear" (28K)
- Same quality content, 6x worse performance
- Urgency > academic framing for titles

### 3. Face-Focused Thumbnails
- VidIQ scores higher but views lower
- Map/evidence thumbnails match audience expectations
