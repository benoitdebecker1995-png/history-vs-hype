# Topic Testing Scorecard - Experimentation Framework

**Philosophy:** Your 1,905-view "winner" isn't success - it's a data point. This scorecard prioritizes systematic experimentation over formula repetition.

**Goal:** Find what breaks 5K+ views through testing, not guessing.

---

## SCORING FRAMEWORK (0-100 Points)

### Category 1: EXPERIMENTATION VALUE (40 points max)

**Purpose:** Prioritize learning over safe bets

#### Novelty Score (20 points)
- **20 pts:** Completely untested angle (new geographic region, new topic type, new format)
- **15 pts:** Tested once, inconclusive results
- **10 pts:** Tested 2-3 times, mixed results
- **5 pts:** Tested 4+ times, diminishing returns
- **0 pts:** Exact repetition of previous video

#### Hypothesis Testing Value (20 points)
- **20 pts:** Tests critical unknown (Does X topic work? Does Y format scale?)
- **15 pts:** Tests refinement (Can we improve Z performance?)
- **10 pts:** Tests minor variable (Different length, different hook style)
- **5 pts:** Low learning value
- **0 pts:** No clear hypothesis

**Examples:**
- "African territorial dispute" = 20 novelty (untested region) + 20 hypothesis (Does territorial dispute pattern hold in Africa?) = **40/40**
- "Venezuela-Guyana Part 2" = 5 novelty (repetition) + 15 hypothesis (Do sequels work?) = **20/40**

---

### Category 2: BREAKOUT POTENTIAL (30 points max)

**Purpose:** Identify topics that could hit 5K+ views

#### Modern Hook Strength (15 points)
- **15 pts:** Active 2025 conflict/crisis (happening NOW)
- **12 pts:** Ongoing 2024-2025 tension (simmering issue)
- **8 pts:** Recent development (2023-2024 event with 2025 relevance)
- **4 pts:** Historical event with weak 2025 connection
- **0 pts:** No modern hook

#### Search Demand / Trend Signal (15 points)
- **15 pts:** Trending topic (news cycle, social media buzz, viral moment)
- **12 pts:** Steady search volume (perennial interest)
- **8 pts:** Niche interest (dedicated audience)
- **4 pts:** Low search volume
- **0 pts:** No discernible demand

**Examples:**
- "JD Vance fact-check #2" = 15 modern (2025 political figure) + 12 trend (VP candidate, ongoing interest) = **27/30**
- "Cambodia-Thailand 2025 border clash" = 15 modern (if 2025 clash exists) + 8 trend (niche) = **23/30**

---

### Category 3: RISK MITIGATION (30 points max)

**Purpose:** Avoid complete failures while still experimenting

#### Anti-Pattern Avoidance (15 points)
- **15 pts:** Avoids ALL known failure patterns
- **10 pts:** Avoids most failure patterns (1 risk factor)
- **5 pts:** Contains 2 failure patterns
- **0 pts:** Contains 3+ failure patterns

**Known Failure Patterns from Data:**
- ❌ No 2024-2025 hook
- ❌ Pure historical analysis (no modern relevance)
- ❌ >13 minutes length
- ❌ Topics with <500 impressions history (algorithm doesn't care)
- ❌ Overly academic framing (archaeological, scholarly)

#### Evidence Availability (15 points)
- **15 pts:** Abundant primary sources (maps, documents, data)
- **12 pts:** Good sources available (2-3 key documents)
- **8 pts:** Moderate sources (need research)
- **4 pts:** Scarce sources (risky)
- **0 pts:** No clear evidence path

**Examples:**
- "Political fact-check with primary sources" = 15 anti-pattern (has 2025 hook, <10 min, evidence-based) + 15 evidence (documents exist) = **30/30**
- "Historical Viking analysis" = 0 anti-pattern (no 2025 hook, academic) + 8 evidence (archaeology) = **8/30**

---

## TOTAL SCORE INTERPRETATION

### 80-100: GREEN LIGHT - High-Value Experiment
- Strong experimentation value + breakout potential + low risk
- **Action:** Prioritize for production
- **Expected outcome:** 500-2,000 views + valuable learning
- **Example:** African territorial dispute with 2025 hook

### 60-79: YELLOW LIGHT - Promising Test
- Good experiment OR good breakout potential, some risks
- **Action:** Produce if capacity allows
- **Expected outcome:** 200-1,000 views + moderate learning
- **Example:** Political fact-check #2 (low novelty but high potential)

### 40-59: ORANGE LIGHT - Speculative Bet
- Either high-risk experiment OR safe topic with low learning
- **Action:** Only if testing specific hypothesis
- **Expected outcome:** 50-500 views + specific insight
- **Example:** 12-minute video testing length hypothesis

### <40: RED LIGHT - Skip or Redesign
- Low learning + low potential + high risk
- **Action:** Redesign or skip entirely
- **Expected outcome:** <100 views, wasted effort
- **Example:** Pure historical analysis without 2025 hook

## HOW TO USE THIS SCORECARD

**Step 1:** Score every video idea before production
**Step 2:** Prioritize GREEN LIGHT experiments (80+)
**Step 3:** Track results in Experiment Log
**Step 4:** Update scoring criteria based on learnings
**Step 5:** Avoid repeating same experiments (diminishing returns)

---

## SUCCESS = LEARNING, NOT VIEWS

**Remember:**
- 1,905 views isn't success - it's a data point
- Each video is an experiment
- Track what you learn, not just what you earn
- 5 experiments teaching different lessons > 5 repetitions of same formula

**Your goal:** Find the topic/format/angle that breaks 5K views through systematic testing, not lucky repetition.
