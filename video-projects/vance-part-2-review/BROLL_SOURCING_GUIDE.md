## 🎯 ESSENTIAL DOCUMENTS (MUST HAVE)

### **CLAIM 1: HUMAN RIGHTS**

#### **1. Mirari Vos (1832) - Pope Gregory XVI**
**What you need:** Section 14 condemning "liberty of conscience"

**Source:** Papal Encyclicals Online
- **URL:** https://www.papalencyclicals.net/greg16/g16mirar.htm
- **What to do:**
  - Scroll to Section 14
  - Screenshot the paragraph starting with "This shameful font of indifferentism"
  - Save as: `mirari-vos-section-14.png`

**Alternative source:**
- Vatican Archives: https://www.vatican.va/content/gregory-xvi/en/documents/enciclica-mirari-vos-15-agosto-1832.html

#### **2. Syllabus of Errors (1864) - Pope Pius IX**
**What you need:** Errors 15, 77, and 78 listed

**Source:** Papal Encyclicals Online
- **URL:** https://www.papalencyclicals.net/Pius09/p9syll.htm
- **What to do:**
  - Find Error 15: "Every man is free to embrace and profess that religion which, guided by the light of reason, he shall consider true"
  - Find Error 77: About Catholic-only states
  - Find Error 78: About allowing other religions
  - Screenshot each or create a simple list graphic
  - Save as: `syllabus-of-errors-15-77-78.png`

#### **3. Dignitatis Humanae (1965) - Vatican II**
**What you need:** Declaration on Religious Freedom

**Source:** Vatican Official Website
- **URL:** https://www.vatican.va/archive/hist_councils/ii_vatican_council/documents/vat-ii_decl_19651207_dignitatis-humanae_en.html
- **What to do:**
  - Screenshot title page
  - Screenshot key paragraph about right to religious liberty
  - Save as: `dignitatis-humanae-1965.png`

## 💡 PRO TIPS

### **For Documents:**
- **Show the URL** in your video (papalencyclicals.net, founders.archives.gov)
- This proves you're not making it up
- Builds trust with skeptical viewers

### **For Graphics:**
- Use high contrast (black text on white, or vice versa)
- Keep text LARGE (readable on mobile)
- Simple > fancy

### **For Portraits:**
- Wikimedia Commons = free, legal, high quality
- Always check "Public Domain" filter
- Download highest resolution available
