- Issued by: Pope Alexander VI

- ✅ Part of broader "Doctrine of Discovery" authorizing colonization

**Follow-up:**

- Protestant powers (England, Netherlands) and Catholic France rejected this division

**Source:** Multiple academic sources, Wikipedia, Britannica

### ✅ ASSESSMENT:
**COMPLETELY ACCURATE.** This was indeed "the big one" dividing the New World.
