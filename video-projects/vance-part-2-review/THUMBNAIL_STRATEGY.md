## 🎨 DESIGN SPECIFICATIONS

### **Canvas Size:**
- **1280 x 720 pixels** (YouTube standard)
- **Resolution:** 300 DPI
- **Format:** PNG (better quality than JPG)

### **Text Guidelines:**
- **Main text:** 60-80pt font minimum
- **Secondary text:** 40-50pt font
- **Font:** Bold, sans-serif (Arial Black, Impact, Bebas Neue)
- **Outline:** 10-15px black stroke around white text (or vice versa)

### **Face Guidelines:**
- **Minimum size:** 30-40% of thumbnail
- **Eye line:** Upper third of image (draws attention)
- **Expression:** Clear and exaggerated (visible at small size)
- **Lighting:** Bright, even, no shadows

### **Color Psychology:**
- **Red/Orange:** Urgency, controversy, attention
- **Blue:** Trust, authority, calm
- **Yellow:** Caution, highlight, curiosity
- **White/Black:** Clarity, contrast, readability

## 🛠️ HOW TO CREATE (Step-by-Step)

### **Option 1: Photoshop (Professional)**

**Concept 3 Tutorial:**
1. Create new file: 1280 x 720, 300 DPI
2. Add your photo:
   - Drag in photo of you with skeptical expression
   - Position left-center, 40-50% of frame
   - Use "Select Subject" → Refine Edge → Cut out background
3. Add background:
   - Fill layer: Bright red (#FF4444) or orange (#FF8C00)
   - Or use gradient: Red to dark red
4. Add top quote box:
   - Rectangle: White fill, black 10px stroke
   - Position top-center, 80% width
   - Add text: "LIBERTY OF CONSCIENCE IS ABSURD"
   - Font: Impact or Arial Black, 70pt, black
   - Below: "- POPE GREGORY XVI, 1832" (50pt)
5. Add bottom text box:
   - Same style as top
   - Text: "VANCE SAYS CHRISTIANITY INVENTED HUMAN RIGHTS. REALLY?"
   - Font: 60pt
6. Add black outline to your photo:
   - Layer Style → Stroke → 15px black
7. Export: File → Export → PNG

**Time:** 15-20 minutes

## 💡 PRO TIPS

### **Thumbnail Psychology:**
1. **Faces beat text** - Include your face, make it big
2. **Curiosity gaps work** - Don't give everything away
3. **Contrast is king** - Bold colors, readable text
4. **Emotion sells** - Shock, skepticism, curiosity in your expression
5. **Mobile-first** - 70% of views are mobile, test at small size

### **Common Mistakes:**
- ❌ Too much text (max 10-15 words)
- ❌ Face too small (needs 30-40% of frame)
- ❌ Low contrast colors (hard to read)
- ❌ Generic stock images (use real documents/your face)
- ❌ Fancy fonts (hard to read at small size)
