## 🔴 CRITICAL B-ROLL DOWNLOADS (15-20 minutes)

### 1. 1893 Ottoman Empire Map (MOST CRITICAL)
**Source:** Gallica (French National Library)
**Direct URL:** https://gallica.bnf.fr/ark:/12148/btv1b530068545
**Resolution:** 10,638 × 7,775 pixels (12.61 MB)
**Format:** JPEG

**Download Steps:**
1. Click the URL above
2. Click download icon (arrow pointing down)
3. Select "Image" format
4. Choose "Full image in its entirety"
5. Download as JPEG
6. Save as: `1893_Ottoman_Map_Iraq.jpg`

---

### 2. Sykes-Picot Agreement Map (1916 Original Signed)
**Source:** Wikimedia Commons
**Direct URL:** https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:MPK1-426_Sykes_Picot_Agreement_Map_signed_8_May_1916.jpg
**Resolution:** 3,322 × 3,225 pixels (5.7 MB)

**Download Steps:**
1. Click the URL
2. Click "Original file" link (below preview image)
3. Right-click on full-size image → Save Image As
4. Save as: `Sykes_Picot_1916_Original.jpg`

**Alternative (Modern Clean Version):**
https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Sykes-Picot.svg (SVG vector - infinitely scalable)

### 3. Modern Middle East Map (Syria, Iraq, Jordan, Lebanon)
**Source:** Wikimedia Commons
**Direct URL:** https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Syria-Iraq-Lebanon_location_map.svg
**Format:** SVG vector

**Download Steps:**
1. Click the URL
2. Download SVG file
3. **If DaVinci won't import SVG:**

### 4. McMahon-Hussein Correspondence (October 24, 1915)
**Source:** Internet Archive
**Direct URL:** https://archive.org/details/mcmahon5957
**Format:** Full document (PDF + JPEG pages)

**Download Steps:**
1. Click the URL
2. Click "Download Options" (right sidebar)
3. Download as PDF or select JPEG pages
4. **Key letter to extract:** October 24, 1915 (page showing the promise quote)
5. Save as: `McMahon_Hussein_1915_Oct24.pdf`

**Text to show on screen:**
> "Subject to the above modifications, Great Britain is prepared to recognise and support the independence of the Arabs in all the regions within the limits demanded by the Sherif of Mecca."

### 5. Balfour Declaration (November 2, 1917)
**Source:** British Library Images Online
**Direct URL:** https://imagesonline.bl.uk
**Resolution:** 2000px free download

**Download Steps:**
1. Go to British Library Images Online
2. Search: "Balfour Declaration"
3. Select the 1917 document image
4. Choose "Free Download" option in dropdown
5. Download as JPEG (2000px)
6. Save as: `Balfour_Declaration_1917.jpg`

**Credit Required (when shown):** © British Library Board

**Text to show on screen:**
> "His Majesty's Government view with favour the establishment in Palestine of a national home for the Jewish people."
