## CRITICAL PRIORITY (Must Have)

### 1. DeConcini Reservation + Treaty Texts — Primary Documents

**[P1] Neutrality Treaty (1977) + Resolution of Ratification**
- **Where:** Congress.gov / State Department Treaty Series
- **What:** Full treaty text + all reservations (DeConcini, Leadership Amendment, conditions)
- **Key content:** Senate Executive Document 95-14(B)
- **Cost:** Free (public domain)

**[P3] Congressional Record — Senate Debates, March-April 1978**
- **Where:** Congress.gov / GPO archives
- **What:** DeConcini's floor speech, opposition arguments, vote records
- **Key dates:** March 15 (DeConcini adoption, 75-23), March 16 (treaty ratification, 68-32)
- **Cost:** Free

**[P4] Hay-Bunau-Varilla Treaty (1903)**
- **Where:** Yale Avalon Project (confirmed available)
- **What:** Complete treaty text, especially Articles II, III, V, XIV, XXIII
- **Key phrase:** "as if it were the sovereign"
- **Cost:** Free

---

### 2. Diplomatic History — University Press Monographs

**[A1] LaFeber, Walter. *The Panama Canal: The Crisis in Historical Perspective.***
- **Publisher:** Oxford University Press (expanded edition)
- **Author credentials:** Marie Underhill Noll Professor of American History, Cornell University
- **Why:** THE definitive diplomatic history. Covers 1903 through 1977-78 debate. Includes treaty texts in appendix.
- **Estimated cost:** $30-50
- **Availability:** Widely available used/new

**[A2] Jorden, William J. *Panama Odyssey.***
- **Publisher:** University of Texas Press, 1984
- **Author credentials:** Former US Ambassador to Panama (1974-78), present during negotiations
- **Why:** First-hand account of treaty negotiations. Behind-the-scenes on DeConcini crisis — Carter's suggestion for Torrijos counter-reservation. White House perspective.
- **Estimated cost:** $30-60 (used)
- **Availability:** Used market, university libraries

---

### 3. Legal Analysis — The Central Question

**[A3] Maier, Harold G. "The Right to Defend the Panama Canal."**
- **Publisher:** *Georgia Journal of International and Comparative Law* (peer-reviewed)
- **Author credentials:** Vanderbilt University Law School
- **Why:** Direct legal analysis of DeConcini Reservation and intervention rights — this is the central legal question of the entire video
- **Estimated cost:** $0-30 (journal access via HeinOnline/JSTOR)
- **Availability:** Law library databases

- **What:** Declassified Carter-era documents from treaty negotiations
- **Why:** Primary sources showing behind-the-scenes US strategy on DeConcini

### 5. 1989 Invasion — Casualty Disputes

**[A5] Physicians for Human Rights. "Operation Just Cause: The Human Cost of Military Action in Panama." 1991.**
- **Where:** PHR website / academic databases
- **What:** Independent civilian casualty investigation
- **Why:** Pentagon says 202 civilians, independent sources say up to 3,000 — need credible independent figure
- **Cost:** Free (PDF)

### 6. Operation Just Cause — Military/Legal Analysis

- **Where:** DTIC (Defense Technical Information Center) — free
- **What:** Analysis of planning and legal justification
- **Why:** Verify whether DeConcini Reservation was explicitly cited in legal basis
- **Cost:** Free

**[A8] Joint Chiefs of Staff. "Operation JUST CAUSE: The Planning and Execution."**
- **Where:** Available as PDF from military archives
- **What:** Official military account
- **Cost:** Free

## MEDIUM PRIORITY

### 7. Bunau-Varilla and 1903 Context

**[A6] McCullough, David. *The Path Between the Seas: The Creation of the Panama Canal, 1870-1914.***
- **Publisher:** Simon & Schuster, 1977 (Pulitzer Prize winner)
- **NOTE:** Not university press — supplementary source
- **Why:** Best biographical source on Bunau-Varilla, French canal project, 1903 independence
- **Key claim to verify:** Bunau-Varilla hadn't been to Panama in 17 years (since 1887)
- **Estimated cost:** $15-25
- **Availability:** Very widely available

### 8. Torrijos Counter-Reservation Legal Effect

**[A9] Academic articles on treaty reservation law**
- **Search:** JSTOR / HeinOnline for "DeConcini reservation" + "Torrijos counter-reservation"
- **What:** Whether counter-reservations legally nullify or modify the original reservation
- **Why:** Critical contested claim — legal scholars appear divided
- **Estimated cost:** $0-30 (database access)

### 9. OAS Condemnation of 1989 Invasion

**[A10] OAS Resolution on Operation Just Cause (December 1989)**
- **Where:** OAS Digital Library
- **What:** Resolution "regretting military intervention" (20-1, 6 abstentions)
- **Cost:** Free
