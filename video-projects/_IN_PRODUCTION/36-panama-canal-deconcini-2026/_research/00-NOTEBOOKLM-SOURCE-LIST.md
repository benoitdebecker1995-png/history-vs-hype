## PRIMARY SOURCES (Essential)

### [P1] Treaty Concerning the Permanent Neutrality and Operation of the Panama Canal (1977)
**What:** The Neutrality Treaty — DeConcini Reservation is attached to THIS treaty
**Why:** Core treaty that remains in effect "in perpetuity" (unlike the Canal Treaty which expired 1999)
**Where to find:**
- US State Department Treaty Series
- Library of Congress
- Congress.gov

**Priority:** CRITICAL
**Format:** PDF of full treaty text + Resolution of Ratification with all reservations
**Estimated cost:** Free (public domain)

**NotebookLM upload name:** `[P1] Neutrality-Treaty-1977.pdf`
