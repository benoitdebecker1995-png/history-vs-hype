## PRIORITY 1: THE PAPYRUS JARF (MUST HAVE)

### Source 1: Pierre Tallet's Publication
**Title:** "Les papyrus de la Mer Rouge I: Le 'journal de Merer' (Papyrus Jarf A et B)"
**Author:** Pierre Tallet
**Published:** 2017, MIFAO 136
**What it contains:**
- Full photographs of Papyrus Jarf
- Complete translations (French, but translatable)
- Archaeological context of Wadi al-Jarf discovery
- Analysis of Merer's entries

**How to access:**
- IFAO (Institut français d'archéologie orientale): https://www.ifao.egnet.net/
- Academic library access
- Google Scholar may have excerpts
- Search: "Tallet Merer papyrus" or "papyrus Jarf"

**What to extract:**
- [ ] Exact text of key diary entries (translated)
- [ ] Reference to "noble Khufu"
- [ ] Descriptions of workforce organization
- [ ] Ration/supply details
- [ ] Dating evidence
