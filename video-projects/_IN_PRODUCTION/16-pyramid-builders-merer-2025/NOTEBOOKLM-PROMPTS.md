## PHASE 2A: PRIMARY SOURCE VERIFICATION

### Prompt 1: Diary Content
```
What are the exact contents of the Diary of Merer (Papyrus Jarf)?

Please provide:
1. Direct quotes from the translated entries
2. What activities does Merer describe?
3. What does the diary say about workforce organization?
4. Any mentions of Khufu or the pyramid by name
5. Page/source citations for each quote
```

### Prompt 2: Dating Evidence
```
How is the Diary of Merer dated to the reign of Khufu (c. 2560 BC)?

What evidence confirms:
1. The papyrus is from the 4th Dynasty
2. It relates to Great Pyramid construction specifically
3. The timeframe is contemporary with the pyramids (not later)

Provide specific evidence and citations.
```

### Prompt 3: Merer's Role
```
Who was Merer based on the papyrus evidence?

1. What was his title/position?
2. What were his responsibilities?
3. How many workers did he oversee?
4. What logistics did he manage?

Use only information from the primary sources.
```
