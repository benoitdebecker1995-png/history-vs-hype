## NOTEBOOKLM PROMPTS

### Prompt 1: The Legal Mechanism

```
I'm making a video about HOW Czechoslovakia was legally dissolved, not WHY.

From these sources, extract:
1. The exact text or summary of Constitutional Act 542/1992 (the dissolution act)
2. What Article 3 specifically said about flags/symbols
3. The exact date and time the dissolution took effect
4. Who signed the final documents

Format as direct quotes with page numbers where available.
```

### Prompt 2: The Asset Division "Spreadsheet"

```
Extract all specific details about how Czechoslovakia divided its assets:

1. What was the exact population ratio used? (2:1?)
2. List everything divided by this ratio (army, debt, gold, art, etc.)
3. What was divided by "territoriality" (location) instead?
4. Were there any disputes? What lasted until 1999?

I need SPECIFIC NUMBERS and EXAMPLES for B-roll graphics.
```

### Prompt 3: The Currency "Delete Button"

```
Extract the timeline and mechanism of the currency separation:

1. When exactly did the old koruna stop being valid?
2. How did the stamp system work? Which denominations?
3. Who printed the stamps? (I heard Canadian Banknote Company for Slovakia)
4. How many banknotes were stamped?
5. When did new banknotes replace the stamped ones?

I need this for a "here's how they actually did it" segment.
```

### Prompt 4: The 9-Year Cleanup

```
The video will argue that dissolution took "31 days" on paper but 9 years in reality.

Extract all post-1993 settlement dates:
- Border finalization (1996?)
- University arrangements (1998?)
- Dual citizenship (1999?)
- Gold reserves (1999?)
- Any other settlements

Format as a timeline I can show on screen.
```

### Prompt 5: Smoking Gun Quotes

```
Find the most quotable lines from these sources that I can display on screen:

1. Any quote from Václav Klaus or Vladimír Mečiar about the split
2. Any quote from Václav Havel (he opposed it)
3. Any quote describing the "no civil war, just lawyers" nature
4. Any quote comparing it to Yugoslavia

Format: "[Quote]" - [Person], [Source], [Page number if available]
```

### Prompt 6: What Makes This Unique

```
What made the Czechoslovak dissolution different from:
- Yugoslavia (violent)
- Soviet Union (chaotic)
- Other post-communist breakups

Extract specific comparisons or scholarly analysis of WHY this was peaceful.
```
