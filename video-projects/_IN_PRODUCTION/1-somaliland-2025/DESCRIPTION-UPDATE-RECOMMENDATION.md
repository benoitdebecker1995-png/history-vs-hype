## For FUTURE Videos (Learnings Applied)

### Pre-Production Checklist Addition:
- [ ] Check for "Article 10 type" gaps — promises/safeguards that were never implemented
- [ ] Include genocide/atrocity context when covering territorial disputes with violent history
- [ ] Cite both Cotran AND Contini for Somaliland legal arguments

### Research Phase:
- When covering legal unions/treaties, always ask:
  - What safeguards were promised?
  - Were they implemented?
  - What happened to them?

### Script Phase:
- The "broken promises" angle is often stronger than procedural defects
- Audiences understand "they promised X and never delivered" better than "the decree-law lapsed under Article 63(3)"
