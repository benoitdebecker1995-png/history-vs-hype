## Research Questions

### The 1960 Paradox
1. Exactly how many countries recognized Somaliland between June 26 - July 1, 1960?
2. What was the mechanism of recognition? (Diplomatic notes? UN votes?)
3. Was Somaliland admitted to the UN during those 5 days?
4. What happened to those recognitions after the merger?

### The Act of Union
1. Was the Act of Union ever ratified by Somaliland's legislature?
2. What does "ratification" require under international law?
3. What were the terms of the merger?
4. Were there protests or dissent at the time?

### The 1991 Restoration
1. What exactly did the 1991 Burao Conference declare?
2. How does "restoration of independence" differ legally from "secession"?
3. What was the legal continuity argument?
4. How does this compare to Baltic states' restoration?

### The AU Position
1. What did the 2005 AU fact-finding mission actually conclude?
2. Why hasn't the AU acted on its own report?
3. What is the AU's stated reason for non-recognition?
4. How does Somaliland compare to South Sudan (which was recognized)?

### Ethiopia MOU 2024
1. What are the exact terms of the MOU?
2. What did Ethiopia offer? (Recognition, investment, what else?)
3. What does Somaliland give in return? (Port access, naval base?)
4. What was Somalia's response?
5. What is the current status (as of Dec 2025)?
