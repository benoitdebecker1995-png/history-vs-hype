# Project 37: Untranslated Evidence — Vichy Statut des Juifs

**Series:** Untranslated Evidence (Pilot Episode)
**Status:** ✅ READY TO FILM — Script complete, fact-check APPROVED

## Concept

Take the Vichy France "Statut des Juifs" (October 3, 1940), translate it clause-by-clause from the original French, and show viewers what it actually says vs. what English-language sources commonly claim.

**Format:** Split-screen — original French document on left, English translation on right, clause-by-clause highlighting. 8-12 minutes.

**Core question:** "What does France's first anti-Jewish statute actually say, and who wrote it?"

## Why This Document

- **Language gap confirmed:** No freely available full English translation found (searched USHMM, Yad Vashem, Avalon Project, Internet History Sourcebooks, academic databases). Wikipedia/USHMM describe provisions in summary only.
- **Source accessible:** Full French text on [Légifrance](https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/jorf/id/JORFTEXT000000869361/) and [French Wikisource](https://fr.wikisource.org/wiki/Loi_du_3_octobre_1940_portant_statut_des_Juifs)
- **Right length:** ~10 articles — perfect for 10-minute clause-by-clause walkthrough
- **Massive search interest:** WW2 + Holocaust + French collaboration = top-tier YouTube topic cluster
- **Strong myth-bust:** "Vichy cooperated under German duress" → France wrote this INDEPENDENTLY, BEFORE any German demand. Pétain's annotated draft (found 2010) shows him personally TIGHTENING restrictions.

## Key Reveals (Article by Article)

- **Article 1:** Biological racial definition of "Jewish" — 3 grandparents of "Jewish race," or 2 if spouse also Jewish. France wrote its OWN racial classification.
- **Articles 2-5:** Systematic professional exclusion — government, military, media, judiciary, banking, teaching. Scope is comprehensive.
- **Article 6 (if present):** Limited exceptions (literary/scientific merit, services to France) — shows the law was designed to appear "reasonable" while being total.
- **Article 9:** Extends to Algeria, colonies, protectorates, mandates — reveals colonial dimension most English sources ignore.
- **Pétain draft (2010 discovery):** Handwritten annotations EXPANDING who counts as Jewish and ADDING professions to the ban. Destroys "bureaucrats did it" defense.
