## PRIMARY DOCUMENTS (MUST HAVE)

### Document 1: Operation Condor Founding Charter
**What it is:** Official founding document signed November 28, 1975
**Best source:** **National Security Archive** (highly recommended)
- Website: nsarchive.gwu.edu
- Search: "Operation Condor founding charter" OR "November 28 1975 Santiago"
- Direct: Look for PDF titled "ACTA DE CLAUSURA" or "Closing Statement"
- License: Public domain (declassified U.S. government document)
- Quality: Usually high-resolution scans

**Alternative sources (if NSA unavailable):**
- **Kornbluh, Peter. *The Pinochet File***
  - University library access
  - Contains document facsimiles
  - Citation: pp. 365-367
  - Quality: Good (published reproduction)

- **McSherry, J. Patrice. *Predatory States***
  - University press
  - Contains Operation Condor documents
  - Quality: Academic quality

**Usage:** Shot 10 (3:45-4:30) - Display document on screen
**Display time:** 15-20 seconds
**Resolution needed:** 1920x1080 minimum
**Format:** PNG or high-resolution JPG

**How to use:**
1. Find PDF of founding charter
2. Screenshot or export individual pages
3. Crop to clean, readable area
4. Import to DaVinci as background or image layer
5. Display for 15+ seconds while narrating
