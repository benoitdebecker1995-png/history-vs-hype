3. **The supreme irony angle**
   - Modern flat earthers using anticlerical propaganda
   - Most articles miss this connection
   - Perfect for "history → modern consequence" formula

4. **Manuscript URLs as differentiator**
   - Show viewers WHERE to verify claims themselves
   - "Here's the link to the Bede manuscript, go check it yourself"
   - Democratizing access to primary sources

```
I went through the medieval manuscripts—Bede from 725 AD, Sacrobosco's university textbook from 1230—and traced the lie to three specific men who invented it between 1828 and 1896.

Here's the evidence. Here's the fabrication. And here's why this 19th-century propaganda is still doing damage in 2025.
```
