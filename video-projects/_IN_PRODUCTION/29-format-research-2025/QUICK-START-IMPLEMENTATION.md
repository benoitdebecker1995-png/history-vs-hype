## STEP 2: Set Up Production Template (Today - 1 hour)

### A. Script Template (Copy-Paste Ready)

**File:** `BOTH-EXTREMES-SCRIPT-TEMPLATE.md`

```markdown
# [TOPIC]: Both Extremes Are Wrong
## Episode [X] - [Date]

---

### ACT 1: THE FALSE BINARY (2 minutes)

**OPENING HOOK (15 seconds):**
"Online, people argue about [TOPIC]. One side says [EXTREME A CLAIM]. The other side says [EXTREME B CLAIM]. I looked at the primary sources. They're both wrong."

**SHOW EVIDENCE OF POLARIZATION (30 seconds):**
[Screenshots of social media posts, YouTube comments, articles from BOTH perspectives]

**STAKES (1 minute):**
- Why does this debate matter?
- What modern consequences does the myth have?
- Why should viewers care?

**TRANSITION:** "Let's look at what the sources actually say."

---

### ACT 2: DEBUNK EXTREME A (3-4 minutes)

**STATE THE CLAIM:**
"Extreme A says: [EXACT CLAIM WITH SOURCE - where do they say this?]"

**SHOW PRIMARY SOURCE #1:**
[Display document on screen]
**Citation:** [Author, Title, Page Number]
**Quote:** "[EXACT TEXT]"

**EXPLAIN DISCREPANCY:**
[Why does Extreme A's claim contradict this source?]
[What are they misquoting/misinterpreting/anachronistically applying?]

**ACADEMIC QUOTE:**
"According to [HISTORIAN] in [BOOK], page [X]: '[EXACT QUOTE DEBUNKING EXTREME A]'"

---

### ACT 3: DEBUNK EXTREME B (3-4 minutes)

**STATE THE CLAIM:**
"Extreme B says: [EXACT CLAIM WITH SOURCE]"

**SHOW PRIMARY SOURCE #2:**
[Display document on screen]
**Citation:** [Author, Title, Page Number]
**Quote:** "[EXACT TEXT]"

**EXPLAIN DISCREPANCY:**
[Why does Extreme B's claim contradict this source?]

**ACADEMIC QUOTE:**
"[HISTORIAN], [BOOK], page [X]: '[EXACT QUOTE DEBUNKING EXTREME B]'"

---

### ACT 4: THE ACTUAL TRUTH (2-3 minutes)

**ACADEMIC CONSENSUS:**
"So what DO the sources show? [SUMMARY OF REAL EVIDENCE]"

**SHOW PRIMARY SOURCES:**
[Display timeline, map, or compilation of evidence]

**WHY IT'S MORE COMPLEX:**
[Explain why reality is more nuanced/boring than either myth]

**KRAUT-STYLE CAUSAL CHAIN:**
"[EVENT A] happened, which meant [CONSEQUENCE B], consequently [RESULT C]..."

**MODERN LESSON:**
"The real story isn't about [DRAMATIC VILLAIN]. It's about [ACTUAL SYSTEMIC CAUSE]. Which, if we're honest, is a lesson we should pay attention to."

---

### OUTRO (30 seconds)

"If you want more myth-busting with primary sources, check out [NEXT EPISODE IN SERIES]. And let me know in the comments: what topic should I cover next in Both Extremes Are Wrong?"

**END SCREEN:**
```

## STEP 6: Measure & Iterate

### After Episode 1 Publishes:

**Track These Metrics (YouTube Studio Analytics):**

1. **Retention Graph:**
   - Where do viewers drop off?
   - Does split-screen source reveal (Act 2-3) hold attention?
   - Is Act 4 (real story) keeping viewers?

2. **Average View Duration:**
   - Target: 4:00+ (40% of 10-min video)
   - If lower: tighten pacing, add pattern interrupts

3. **CTR (Click-Through Rate):**
   - Target: 6-8%
   - If lower: test new thumbnail variations

4. **Comments:**
   - Are both sides engaging?
   - Any constructive debate?
   - Questions for future episodes?

### Iterate Based on Data:

**If retention drops at Act 2-3 (source display):**
- Add more visual variety (zooms, highlights, animations)
- Shorten source segments
- Add music/sound effects for pacing

**If comments are hostile/partisan:**
- Double down on "evidence-based" framing in next episode
- Consider pinning constructive comment
- Respond to good-faith questions only

**If views underperform (<500 first week):**
- Improve thumbnail (test 2-3 variations)
- Share more widely (Reddit, Twitter)
- Check title SEO (add "fact check" or "debunked")

## CRITICAL SUCCESS FACTORS

### Do These Things:
✅ **Show sources on screen** (not just narrate)
✅ **Use exact quotes** with page numbers
✅ **Maintain neutral tone** (you're investigating, not prosecuting)
✅ **Acknowledge what each side gets right** (intellectual honesty)
✅ **Release consistently** (every 2 weeks builds expectation)

### Don't Do These Things:
❌ **Don't narrate sources** without showing them
❌ **Don't take partisan stance** (you're debunking BOTH extremes)
❌ **Don't oversimplify** the real story (complexity is the point)
❌ **Don't skip citations** (every claim needs source)
❌ **Don't batch production** (finish one before starting next)
