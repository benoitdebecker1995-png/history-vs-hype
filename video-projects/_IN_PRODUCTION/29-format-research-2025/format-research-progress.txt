❌ "Both extremes wrong" as recurring series format
❌ Forensic treaty/legal document line-by-line breakdown
❌ "The Map They Ignored" pattern (alternative borders from real documents)
❌ Comparative timeline (same date, different theaters)

5 SIGNATURE FORMAT TEMPLATES DESIGNED:

1. THE DOCUMENT SHOWDOWN
   - Side-by-side source comparison
   - Split screen: Document A (left) vs Document B (right)
   - Highlight contradictions in real-time
   - 10-12 min format
   - Example: Confederate Constitution vs Cornerstone Speech

2. BOTH EXTREMES ARE WRONG (Series)
   - Systematic myth-busting format
   - Act 1: False binary
   - Act 2: Debunk Extreme A
   - Act 3: Debunk Extreme B
   - Act 4: Actual truth from primary sources
   - 9-11 min format
   - 15 episode ideas identified

3. TREATY AUTOPSY
   - Forensic legal document line-by-line
   - Read treaty on screen, every word
   - Color-code key phrases (yellow), ambiguities (red)
   - Map visuals for each article
   - 12-15 min format
   - Example: Belize-Honduras 1930 Exchange of Notes

4. THE MAP THEY IGNORED
   - Alternative historical borders from real documents
   - Show current borders vs proposed alternatives
   - MUST have documentary evidence
   - Side-by-side comparison
   - 10-12 min format
   - Example: King-Crane Commission 1919 Middle East map

5. SAME DAY, DIFFERENT WAR
   - Comparative timeline analysis
   - Three theaters on same date
   - Synchronized storytelling
   - Shows resource allocation context
   - 8-10 min format
   - Example: June 6, 1944 across all WWII theaters

VALIDATION AGAINST CHANNEL DNA:
✅ All formats emphasize PRIMARY SOURCE DISPLAY
✅ All use forensic/documentary tone
✅ All target educated, intellectually curious audience
✅ All avoid partisan framing
✅ All scalable (10-50+ episodes each)

RECOMMENDED IMPLEMENTATION:
Phase 1: Launch "Both Extremes Are Wrong" series (highest scalability)
Phase 2: Add "Document Showdown" as second series
Phase 3: "Treaty Autopsy" for ICJ cases
