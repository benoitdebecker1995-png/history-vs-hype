## Primary Sources [P]

### [P1] Recopilación de Leyes de los Reynos de las Indias (1681/1756 edition)
- **Type:** Primary legal code
- **Access:** [Internet Archive — Free](https://archive.org/details/recopilaciondele02spai_1) (all 4 volumes available)
- **Key content:** Book IV (colonization, municipal government, land grants), Book VI (Indians)
- **NotebookLM filename:** `[P1] Recopilacion-Tomo-2.pdf` (focus on land grant provisions)
- **Priority:** HIGH — the document itself
- **Note:** In Spanish. Upload relevant sections. Focus on titles governing land distribution, ejidos, and communal rights.

### [P2] Treaty of Guadalupe Hidalgo (1848) — Articles VIII and IX
- **Type:** Treaty text
- **Access:** [Avalon Project — Free](https://avalon.law.yale.edu/19th_century/guadhida.asp)
- **Key content:** Property protection guarantees for Mexican citizens
- **NotebookLM filename:** `[P2] Treaty-Guadalupe-Hidalgo.pdf`
- **Priority:** HIGH — the promise that was broken

### [P3] United States v. Sandoval, 167 U.S. 278 (1897)
- **Type:** Supreme Court opinion
- **Access:** [Justia — Free](https://supreme.justia.com/cases/federal/us/167/278/)
- **Key content:** The ruling that common lands belonged to the sovereign, not the community
- **NotebookLM filename:** `[P3] US-v-Sandoval-1897.pdf`
- **Priority:** HIGH — the smoking gun misreading

### [P4] Lobato v. Taylor, 71 P.3d 938 (Colorado Supreme Court, 2002)
- **Type:** State supreme court opinion
- **Access:** [Justia — Free](https://law.justia.com/cases/colorado/supreme-court/2002/00sc527-0.html)
- **Key content:** Restoration of land grant access rights
- **NotebookLM filename:** `[P4] Lobato-v-Taylor-2002.pdf`
- **Priority:** MEDIUM — modern resolution

### [P5] GAO Report GAO-04-59 (2004)
- **Type:** Government report
- **Access:** [GAO — Free](https://www.gao.gov/assets/a157551.html)
- **Key content:** Findings on land grant claims process failures, 5 options for Congress
- **NotebookLM filename:** `[P5] GAO-04-59-Guadalupe-Hidalgo.pdf`
- **Priority:** HIGH — official US government acknowledgment of failures

### [P6] GAO Report GAO-01-951 (2001)
- **Type:** Government report
- **Access:** [GAO — Free](https://www.gao.gov/assets/gao-01-951.pdf)
- **Key content:** Definition and list of 154 community land grants in NM
- **NotebookLM filename:** `[P6] GAO-01-951-Land-Grants-List.pdf`
- **Priority:** MEDIUM — comprehensive list and definitions

## Academic Sources [A]

### [A1] Ebright, Malcolm. *Land Grants and Lawsuits in Northern New Mexico*
- **Publisher:** University of New Mexico Press, 1994
- **Key chapters:** Case studies of specific land grants and their adjudication by the US
- **Relevance:** Leading scholar on NM land grants; 8 case studies with primary documentation
- **NotebookLM filename:** `[A1] Ebright-Land-Grants-Lawsuits.pdf`
- **Priority:** HIGH — the definitive work on NM land grant loss

### [A2] Ebright, Malcolm (ed.). *Spanish and Mexican Land Grants and the Law*
- **Publisher:** Sunflower University Press, 1989
- **Key chapters:** 8 land grant scholars including Iris Engstrand on California ranchos
- **Relevance:** Legal analysis of how US courts mishandled Spanish/Mexican grants
- **NotebookLM filename:** `[A2] Ebright-Spanish-Mexican-Law.pdf`
- **Priority:** HIGH — legal framework analysis

### [A3] Ebright, Malcolm. *Advocates for the Oppressed: Hispanos, Indians, Genízaros, and Their Land in New Mexico*
- **Publisher:** University of New Mexico Press, 2014
- **Relevance:** Updated scholarship on land grant communities
- **NotebookLM filename:** `[A3] Ebright-Advocates-Oppressed.pdf`
- **Priority:** MEDIUM

### [A4] Westphall, Victor. *Mercedes Reales: Hispanic Land Grants of the Upper Rio Grande Region*
- **Publisher:** University of New Mexico Press, 1983
- **Relevance:** Classic study of Hispanic land grants
- **NotebookLM filename:** `[A4] Westphall-Mercedes-Reales.pdf`
- **Priority:** MEDIUM

### [A5] Van Ness, John R. and Christine M. Van Ness (eds.). *Spanish and Mexican Land Grants in New Mexico and Colorado*
- **Publisher:** Center for Land Grant Studies / Manhattan, Kansas, 1980
- **Relevance:** Early comprehensive study
- **NotebookLM filename:** `[A5] VanNess-Land-Grants-NM-CO.pdf`
- **Priority:** MEDIUM

### [A6] Haring, C.H. *The Spanish Empire in America*
- **Publisher:** Oxford University Press (reprint Harcourt Brace, various editions)
- **Relevance:** Foundational work on Spanish colonial administration including the Laws of the Indies
- **NotebookLM filename:** `[A6] Haring-Spanish-Empire.pdf`
- **Priority:** MEDIUM — for context on how the Recopilación was administered

### [A7] Colorado Law Review article: "The Failings of the United States Justice System: Lobato v. Taylor and Mexican Community Land Grants"
- **Publisher:** University of Colorado Law Review, Vol. 93
- **Access:** [Colorado Law Review](https://lawreview.colorado.edu/print/volume-93/the-failings-of-the-united-states-justice-system-lobato-v-taylor-and-mexican-community-land-grants/)
- **Relevance:** Academic legal analysis of the Lobato case and broader land grant failures
- **NotebookLM filename:** `[A7] Colorado-Law-Review-Lobato.pdf`
- **Priority:** HIGH — connects historical and modern

### [A8] Bowden, J.J. *Private Land Claims in the Southwest* (1969)
- **Access:** [NM DOJ](https://nmdoj.gov/wp-content/uploads/1969_J.-J.-Bowden-Private-Land-Claims-in-the-Southwest.pdf) — Free PDF
- **Relevance:** Detailed case-by-case analysis of private land claims
- **NotebookLM filename:** `[A8] Bowden-Private-Land-Claims.pdf`
- **Priority:** HIGH — case studies with documentation
