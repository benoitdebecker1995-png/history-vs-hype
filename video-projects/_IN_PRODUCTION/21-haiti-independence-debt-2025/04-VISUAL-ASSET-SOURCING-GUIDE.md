- **Wikimedia Commons** - Public domain portraits, maps, historical photos

- **French National Archives (online)** - Some digitized documents

### May Require Purchase/License:
- **Getty Images** - High-quality historical photos
- **Alamy** - Document scans
- **Bridgeman Images** - Art and portraits

### May Require Archive Request:
- **Archives Nationales de France** - Original 1825 documents
- **Haitian National Archives** - Boyer proclamations

- All maps (Canva, MapChart)
