### PRIMARY SOURCES USED:

**1. Korherr Report (March 23, 1943)**
- Nazi SS statistician Dr. Richard Korherr to Heinrich Himmler
- Official title: "Die Endlösung der Judenfrage" (Final Solution to the Jewish Question)
- Documents: 4 million Jews "evacuated" (Nazi euphemism) from 1937-December 1942
- Source: US Holocaust Memorial Museum, German History in Documents database
- Status: ✅ Accessible online (germanhistorydocs.org)

**2. Höfle Telegram (January 11, 1943)**
- Intercepted encrypted Nazi radio message
- SS Sturmbannführer Hermann Höfle to Adolf Eichmann
- Decoded by British intelligence (Enigma)
- Specific death counts for 4 Operation Reinhard camps in 1942:
  - Belzec: 434,508
  - Sobibor: 101,370
  - Treblinka: 71,355
  - Majdanek: 24,733
  - **Total 1942 alone:** 1,274,166
- Discovered 2000 in UK Public Record Office archives
- Status: ✅ Translation available

**3. Auschwitz Crematorium Documentation**
- SS Central Building Administration blueprints
- Karl Bischoff (head architect) to Hans Kammler letter
- Documents 52 crematorium ovens (NOT 15 as Fuentes claims)
- Official capacity calculation (June 28, 1943): 4,416 corpses/day
- Sonderkommando survivor testimony: up to 8,000/day capacity
- Topf & Sons engineering specifications (company that built ovens)
- Status: ✅ Available via Auschwitz-Birkenau Museum, Yad Vashem

**4. Additional Documentary Evidence:**
- Einsatzgruppen reports: Mobile killing units documented 1.3M shot (not cremated)
- Mass graves: Soviet liberation documentation, forensic excavations
- Most victims NOT cremated in ovens (buried in pits)
