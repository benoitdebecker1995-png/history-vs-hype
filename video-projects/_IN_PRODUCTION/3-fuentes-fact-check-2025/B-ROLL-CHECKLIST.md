## EDITING NOTES

### Text Overlay Style
- Font: Clean sans-serif (Helvetica, Arial, Roboto)
- Color: White text, black semi-transparent background
- Size: Legible at 1080p on mobile
- Duration: 3-5 seconds per quote

### Document Display
- Always show: Document name + Date
- Zoom to key statistics/quotes
- Use red circles/arrows sparingly (only for complex documents)
- Let documents speak for themselves

### Split-Screen Format
- 50/50 split for technical segments
- Alternate every 90 seconds
- Talking head on left, document on right (or vice versa)

### Document Montage Pacing
- 2-3 seconds per document
- Ken Burns effect (slow zoom)
- No music during document reveals (let gravity of evidence land)
