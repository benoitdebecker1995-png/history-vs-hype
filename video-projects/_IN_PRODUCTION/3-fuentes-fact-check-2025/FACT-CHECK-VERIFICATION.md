### CLAIM 3: "Auschwitz-Birkenau had 52 crematorium ovens, not 15"

**VERDICT:** ✅ **VERIFIED** (with clarification needed)

**Evidence:**
- Letter dated June 28, 1943, from Karl Bischoff to Hans Kammler
- Bischoff: Head of Central Building Administration, Auschwitz-Birkenau
- **Breakdown of MUFFLES (cremation chambers):**
  - **Birkenau Crematorium II:** 15 muffles (five 3-muffle ovens)
  - **Birkenau Crematorium III:** 15 muffles (five 3-muffle ovens)
  - **Birkenau Crematorium IV:** 8 muffles
  - **Birkenau Crematorium V:** 8 muffles
  - **Subtotal Birkenau:** 46 muffles
  - **Auschwitz I (main camp):** 6 muffles (three double-muffle ovens, built Aug 1940-May 1942)
  - **TOTAL AUSCHWITZ COMPLEX:** 52 muffles

**Official SS Capacity Calculation (June 28, 1943):**
- 4,416 corpses per day (24-hour period)
- Crematorium II: 1,440/day
- Crematorium III: 1,440/day
- Crematorium IV: 768/day
- Crematorium V: 768/day

**Manufacturer:** Topf & Sons (J.A. Topf und Söhne)

**CLARIFICATION NEEDED:**
When saying "52 ovens," technically it's "52 muffles (cremation chambers)" across multiple ovens at both Auschwitz-Birkenau and Auschwitz I main camp. Some sources cite "46 muffles at Birkenau" separately.

**RECOMMENDED SCRIPT LANGUAGE:**
> "Auschwitz-Birkenau alone had 52 crematorium ovens"

Should be:
> "The Auschwitz complex had 52 cremation chambers across multiple crematoria - not the 15 Fuentes claims. SS documents show a capacity of over 4,400 corpses per day."

**Action:** 📝 Clarify "52 muffles/chambers" not "52 ovens" (technical accuracy)
