## CRITICAL SOURCES (Must have before scripting)

### [A1] Jonas, Raymond. *The Battle of Adwa: African Victory in the Age of Empire*
- **Publisher:** Harvard University Press, 2011 (paperback 2014)
- **ISBN:** 9780674052741
- **Why essential:** The definitive English-language scholarly account. Covers the treaty, Antonelli's role, the war, and the battle. Choice Outstanding Academic Book. Toyin Falola Africa Book Award.
- **Key chapters to focus on:** Treaty of Wuchale negotiation, Article 17 discrepancy, Menelik's repudiation, the battle itself
- **Buy at:** [Harvard UP](https://www.hup.harvard.edu/books/9780674503847) or Amazon ($25-30 paperback)
- **NotebookLM filename:** `[A1] Jonas-Battle-of-Adwa-HUP-2011.pdf`

### [A2] Zewde, Bahru. *A History of Modern Ethiopia, 1855–1991* (2nd ed.)
- **Publisher:** Ohio University Press / James Currey, 2002
- **ISBN:** 9780821414408
- **Why essential:** Ethiopian historian — provides the non-European perspective on the treaty and the war. Critical for not presenting only the Italian/European side.
- **Access:** Available on [Internet Archive](https://archive.org/details/historyofmoderne00bahr) (free) AND on JSTOR
- **NotebookLM filename:** `[A2] Zewde-History-Modern-Ethiopia-2002.pdf`

### [A3] Rubenson, Sven. *The Survival of Ethiopian Independence*
- **Publisher:** Heinemann Educational Books, 1976
- **Why essential:** Rubenson is the leading specialist on 19th-century Ethiopian foreign policy. He wrote a dedicated monograph on Article 17 (*Wichale XVII*, 1964). This longer work has the full treaty analysis and diplomatic context.
- **Access:** Library / interlibrary loan (older, may not be online)
- **NotebookLM filename:** `[A3] Rubenson-Survival-Ethiopian-Independence-1976.pdf`

## STRONG SUPPORTING SOURCES

### [A4] Marcus, Harold G. *The Life and Times of Menelik II: Ethiopia 1844-1913*
- **Publisher:** Clarendon Press (Oxford), 1975; Africa World Press reprint, 1995
- **ISBN:** Various
- **Why essential:** **The definitive Menelik biography.** Covers treaty negotiations, Menelik's diplomatic strategy, and the war in full detail.
- **Access:** Amazon (~$25)
- **NotebookLM filename:** `[A4] Marcus-Life-Times-Menelik-1975.pdf`

### [A5] Milkias, Paulos and Metaferia, Getachew (eds.). *The Battle of Adwa: Reflections on Ethiopia's Historic Victory Against European Colonialism*
- **Publisher:** Algora Publishing, 2005
- **Why useful:** Essay collection with 9 scholars including Richard Pankhurst — multiple perspectives on causes, conduct, and legacy
- **Access:** Amazon ($25-35)
- **NotebookLM filename:** `[A5] Milkias-Metaferia-Battle-Adwa-2005.pdf`

### [A6] Prouty, Chris. *Empress Taytu and Menilek II: Ethiopia, 1883-1910*
- **Publisher:** Africa World Press, 1986
- **Why useful:** Empress Taytu commanded her own forces at Adwa (~5,000 infantry + 600 cavalry). This is the only dedicated scholarly account of her role.
- **Access:** Amazon (~$25)
- **NotebookLM filename:** `[A6] Prouty-Empress-Taytu-1986.pdf`

### [A7] McLachlan, Sean. *Armies of the Adowa Campaign 1896*
- **Publisher:** Osprey Publishing
- **Why useful:** Military details — force composition, weapons, tactics
- **Access:** **FREE PDF** at [SA History Online](https://sahistory.org.za/sites/default/files/archive-files/sean_mclachlan_armies_of_the_adowa_campaign_1896book4you.pdf)
- **NotebookLM filename:** `[A7] McLachlan-Armies-Adowa-Osprey.pdf`

## PRIMARY SOURCES (Free, Online)

### [P1] Treaty of Wuchale — Full Text (Italian + English Translation)
- **Language:** Italian (original); English translation available
- **Access:**
  - Italian original: [gspi.unipr.it PDF](https://gspi.unipr.it/sites/gspi/files/allegatiparagrafo/17-02-2015/documenti_-_treaty_of_wuchale_1889.pdf)
  - English translation: [Horn Affairs](https://hornaffairs.com/2011/08/17/text-of-wuchale-treaty-1989-ethio-italian-treaty/)
  - PDF with Articles: [Afro Legends](https://afrolegends.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/10/ethiopie_traite-de-wuchale.pdf)
- **Key focus:** Article 17 — print both Italian and Amharic versions side-by-side
- **NotebookLM filename:** `[P1] Treaty-of-Wuchale-1889-Full-Text.pdf`
- **Status:** DOWNLOAD NOW — free

### [P2] Treaty of Addis Ababa (October 26, 1896)
- **Language:** Italian + Amharic (with English available)
- **Why essential:** This is the document proving Italy fully surrendered — recognizes Ethiopian sovereignty, nullifies Wuchale entirely
- **Access:** Search Avalon Project (Yale Law School) or Internet Archive
- **NotebookLM filename:** `[P2] Treaty-Addis-Ababa-1896.pdf`
- **Status:** LOCATE AND DOWNLOAD

### [P3] Menelik II's Circular Letter to European Monarchs (1891)
- **Language:** French (diplomatic correspondence)
- **Why essential:** Menelik directly told European powers the Italian protectorate claim was false — before the war. This letter exists and is referenced in scholarship.
- **Access:** May be in Ethiopian state archives or reproduced in Rubenson/Jonas
- **NotebookLM filename:** `[P3] Menelik-Circular-Letter-1891.pdf`
- **Status:** CHECK Jonas (A1) — likely reproduced there
