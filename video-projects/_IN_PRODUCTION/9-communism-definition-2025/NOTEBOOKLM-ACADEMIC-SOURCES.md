## TIER 1: PRIMARY SOURCES (MUST ACQUIRE)

### 1. Karl Marx & Friedrich Engels - The Communist Manifesto (1848)

**Why Essential:**
- Need exact Marx quotes for on-screen display
- Shows what Marx ACTUALLY wrote vs. interpretations
- Public domain = free, but need reliable translation

**What To Extract:**
- Opening line: "A spectre is haunting Europe"
- "History of all hitherto existing society is the history of class struggles"
- Definition of bourgeois property abolition
- Transitional policy demands (10 points)
- "Workers of the world, unite!"

**Where To Get:**
- **Best:** Marxists.org - verified translation with notes
- **URL:** https://www.marxists.org/archive/marx/works/1848/communist-manifesto/
- **Alternative:** Project Gutenberg
- **Format:** HTML or PDF (copyable text for NotebookLM)

**How To Use In Video:**
- Display actual text on screen
- "Here's what Marx wrote" segments
- Quote exact language, not paraphrases

### 2. Karl Marx - Das Kapital, Volume 1 (1867)

**Why Essential:**
- Economic theory foundation (labor theory of value, surplus value)
- More detailed than Manifesto
- Need quotes, not just summaries

**What To Extract:**
- **Chapter 1:** Commodities and value (what determines value?)
- **Chapter 7-8:** Labor-power and surplus value (exploitation mechanism)
- **Chapter 26-33:** Primitive accumulation (how capitalism began)
- Commodity fetishism concept
- "Socially necessary labor time" definition

**Where To Get:**
- **Best:** Marxists.org - English translation (Moore & Aveling)
- **URL:** https://www.marxists.org/archive/marx/works/1867-c1/
- **Note:** Full text is long (1,000+ pages), extract key chapters only
- **Format:** HTML or PDF

**How To Use In Video:**
- Technical economic concepts explained
- "Marx's critique of capitalism" segment
- Surplus value animation/visual

### 3. Constitution of the USSR (1936 - Stalin Constitution)

**Why Essential:**
- Shows how Soviet state was officially structured
- Comparison: Marx's theory (stateless) vs. Soviet reality (strong state)
- Primary source for "what USSR claimed to be"

**What To Extract:**
- **Article 1:** "USSR is a socialist state of workers and peasants"
- **Article 4:** "Economic foundation of USSR is socialist system"
- **Article 12:** "Labor is duty and honor of every able-bodied citizen"
- **Articles on Communist Party** (Party's leading role)
- Rights guaranteed vs. actually implemented

**Where To Get:**
- **URL:** https://www.marxists.org/reference/archive/stalin/works/1936/12/05.htm
- **Alternative:** Constitution Society archives
- **Format:** HTML or PDF

**How To Use In Video:**
- Side-by-side: "Constitution said" vs. "Reality was"
- Show Article text on screen
- "Soviet state never withered" segment
