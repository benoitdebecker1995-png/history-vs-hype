## TERRA NULLIUS & INTERNATIONAL LAW

### 8. **Cornell Law: "Terra Nullius" (Legal Definition)**
**Link:** [Cornell Law - Wex](https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/terra_nullius)
**What it explains:**
- Legal definition: "nobody's land"
- In international law, territory which belongs to no state
- Sovereignty can be acquired by occupation

**Use for:** Legal framework for unclaimed land

### 9. **Britannica: "Terra Nullius"**
