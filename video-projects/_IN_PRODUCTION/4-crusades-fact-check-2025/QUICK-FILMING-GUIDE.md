## 🎬 FILMING REMINDERS

**Camera:**
- Eye level
- Look just above lens
- Head and shoulders frame

**Delivery:**
- Conversational (improvise transitions)
- Pause 2 seconds before each quote
- Pause 3 seconds after Niketas quote
- Serious tone (not preachy)
- Geopolitical stakes (Pentagon/recruitment) NOT cultural commentary

**Energy:**
- Hook: High energy, urgent
- Quotes: Slow, clear, let them land
- Modern connection: Serious, balanced
- CTA: Conversational, friendly

**If you mess up:**
- Don't restart
- Pause, collect, continue from last key point
- Edit can fix pauses
