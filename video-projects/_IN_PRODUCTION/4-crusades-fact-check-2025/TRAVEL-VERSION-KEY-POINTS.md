## 🎬 FILMING TIPS (NO AUTO EYE TRACKING)

### Camera Setup:
- Position at eye level
- Frame: Head and shoulders (talking head)
- Look JUST above camera lens (appears like eye contact)

### Delivery Style:
- **Conversational, not scripted** (improvise between key points)
- **Pause before reading quotes** (signals importance)
- **Vary energy:** Serious for evidence, conversational for transitions
- **Hand gestures OK** (more natural without auto-tracking)

### If You Mess Up:
- Don't restart entire video
- Pause, collect thought, continue from last key point
- Edit can cut pauses
- Aim for 70% good, not 100% perfect

### Multiple Takes Strategy:
1. **Take 1:** Get through structure, timing
2. **Take 2:** Tighten delivery, better energy
3. **Take 3:** (if needed) Best quotes, natural flow

Pick best overall take (not perfect take)
