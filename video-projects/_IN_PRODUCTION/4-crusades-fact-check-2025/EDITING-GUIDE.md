# Shot-by-Shot Editing Guide: Crusades Fact-Check

**Project:** 4-crusades-fact-check-2025
**Video Length:** ~11:45 (1,290 words @ 110 wpm)
**Format:** Hybrid talking head (60%) + B-roll evidence (30%) + Pax clips (10%)
**Target Retention:** 35%+ (maintain JD Vance efficiency benchmark)

---

## EDITING PHILOSOPHY

**Visual Rhythm Pattern:**
- **Talking Head** = Making arguments, connecting evidence, authority
- **B-roll** = Evidence, documents, maps (NOT decoration)
- **Pax Clips** = Establishing claims being fact-checked (credibility)

**Rules:**
- Never more than 30 seconds on talking head without visual change
- Primary source quotes: 4-5 seconds minimum (reading time)
- Shocking quotes (Ma'arra): 5-6 seconds (processing time)
- All Pax clips: Keep audio, show full-screen (transparency)

**Music:** Low ambient academic background (NOT epic orchestral)
- Drop music volume during primary source quotes
- Silence during Ma'arra section (7:00-7:50)
