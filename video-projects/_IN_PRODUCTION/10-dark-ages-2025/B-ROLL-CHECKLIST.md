## PRODUCTION NOTES

### Asset Sources Priority:
1. **Wikimedia Commons** (public domain, free to use)
2. **British Museum Online Collection** (many public domain images)
3. **Manuscript databases** (Gallica, e-codices for Carolingian manuscripts)
4. **DaVinci Resolve Text Overlays** (create in-house for data/statistics)

### Text Overlay Style (DaVinci Resolve):
- **Font:** Arial or Helvetica (clean, readable on mobile)
- **Size:** Large enough for mobile viewing
- **Color:** White text on black background (high contrast)
- **Animation:** Simple cuts, no fancy transitions
- **Duration:** 2-4 seconds per frame (readable pace)

### Map Creation:
- Can use PowerPoint with simple shapes
- Export as PNG
- Keep labels large and clear
- Use contrasting colors

### Manuscript Images - Search Terms:
- "Carolingian minuscule manuscript"
- "Tours Gospel"
- "Utrecht Psalter"
- "Carolingian charter"
- "Medieval property document"

### Historical Maps - Search Terms:
- "Visigothic Spain map"
- "Lombard Italy map"
- "Carolingian Empire map"
- "St. Gall monastery location"
