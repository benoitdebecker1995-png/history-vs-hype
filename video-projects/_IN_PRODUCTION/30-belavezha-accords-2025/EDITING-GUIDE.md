## Text Overlay Specifications

### Document Quotes
- **Font:** Serif (Times New Roman or similar) for historical authenticity
- **Background:** Slightly aged paper texture
- **Highlight method:** Yellow/gold underline or background on key phrases

### Title Cards
- **PROSECUTION:** Red accent, serious
- **DEFENSE:** Blue accent, measured
- **VERDICT:** Neutral/gold, authoritative

### Statistics
- **Bar charts:** Clean, minimal, single color
- **Numbers:** Large, bold, easy to read

## Audio Notes

- **Music:** Minimal, documentary-style underscore
- **Transition sounds:** Optional subtle gavel/paper sounds for section changes
- **Pacing:** Let document reads breathe - don't rush text overlays

## Color Grading

- Slightly desaturated for historical/documentary feel
- Warm tones for talking head
- Cooler tones for Soviet-era content overlays
