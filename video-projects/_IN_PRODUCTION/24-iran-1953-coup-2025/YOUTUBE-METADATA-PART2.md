## Description

June 1979: Iranian revolutionaries publish a democratic constitution. Strong parliament. Elected president. Zero supreme leaders.

December 1979: A completely different constitution gets approved—one designed to make sure democracy could never happen.

This video explains exactly how Iran's revolution was hijacked, and the mechanism that has controlled every Iranian election since.

**What you'll learn:**
- How the first draft constitution was replaced with one concentrating all power
- The Guardian Council: 12 people who can disqualify 99% of candidates before anyone votes
- How the IRGC controls 20-40% of the economy, making opposition economically suicidal
- Why both "it's all America's fault" and "it's just a theocracy" miss the point
- The 2009, 2019, 2022, and 2025 protests—same pattern, same response

If you want to understand how a revolution that promised freedom built one of the most sophisticated systems of control in the world, this is the documentary.
