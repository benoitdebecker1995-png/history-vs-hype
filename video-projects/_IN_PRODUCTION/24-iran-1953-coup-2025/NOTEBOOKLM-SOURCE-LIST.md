### 4. Ali Rahnema - "Behind the 1953 Coup in Iran" (2014)
**Publisher:** Cambridge University Press
**ISBN:** 978-1107429758
**Pages:** 280
**Price:** ~$30-40

**Where to buy:**
- Amazon: https://www.amazon.com/Behind-1953-Coup-Iran-Turncoats/dp/1107429757
- Cambridge University Press

**Why essential:**
- Called "the best account of how the coup occurred" by leading expert Mark Gasiorowski
- "Will stand as the definitive work on this subject for many years to come"
- Draws on American, British, AND Iranian sources
- Focuses on the four-day period between failed and successful coups
- MOST RECENT major academic work (2014)

**What you'll get:**
- Day-by-day reconstruction from multiple perspectives
- Iranian internal politics during the coup
- Balance between external and internal factors

**Format:** Buy ebook, convert to PDF
