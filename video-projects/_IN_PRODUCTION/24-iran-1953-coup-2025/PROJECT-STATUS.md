## Video Thesis

**The Question:**
Why does Iran keep exploding—and why does it keep getting crushed?

**The Answer:**
Iran has been trapped in a 120-year cycle: constitutional demands vs. authoritarian suppression. Three times Iranians built democratic institutions. Three times foreign or domestic powers destroyed them. The mechanisms of suppression evolved, but the pattern remained.

**Both Extremes Wrong:**
- EXTREME 1: "Iran is just an oppressive theocracy" (ignores democratic tradition)
- EXTREME 2: "It's all America's fault" (ignores internal mechanisms)
- TRUTH: External intervention AND internal mechanisms created a self-reinforcing cycle
