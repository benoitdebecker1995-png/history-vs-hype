## The Core Myth to Bust

**What people believe:**
"The US-Mexico border was set once and for all by the Treaty of Guadalupe Hidalgo in 1848."

**The reality:**
The treaty's boundary descriptions didn't match actual geography. The border had to be renegotiated just 5 years later (Gadsden Purchase, 1853). Survey teams found the maps were wrong. The "finished" border took decades of additional treaties and disputes to finalize.
