## Priority 1: Core Academic Books

### 1. *The Treaty of Guadalupe Hidalgo: A Legacy of Conflict*
**Author:** Richard Griswold del Castillo
**Publisher:** University of Oklahoma Press (1990)
**Why:** THE foundational scholarly work on this treaty. Provides in-depth analysis of the diplomacy, drafting, and legacy.
**Price:** ~$25-30 (paperback)
**ISBN:** 978-0806122403

### 2. *So Far from God: The U.S. War with Mexico, 1846-1848*
**Author:** John S.D. Eisenhower
**Publisher:** University of Oklahoma Press (2000)
**Why:** Comprehensive military and diplomatic history of the war that led to the treaty. Context for why the treaty was negotiated under pressure.
**Price:** ~$25
**ISBN:** 978-0806132792

### 3. *In the Mean Time: Temporality and Cultural Politics in U.S.-Mexico Relations*
**Author:** Erin Murrah-Mandril
**Publisher:** University of Nebraska Press (2020)
**Why:** 2020 Choice Outstanding Academic Title. Examines how the treaty transferred territory and deferred citizenship.
**Price:** ~$30
**ISBN:** 978-1496211828

---

## Priority 2: Boundary Survey & Gadsden Purchase

### 4. *The Gadsden Treaty*
**Author:** Paul Neff Garber
**Publisher:** University of Pennsylvania Press (1923, reprinted)
**Why:** Classic detailed study of the Gadsden Purchase negotiations and boundary disputes.
**Price:** Variable (may need to find used/archive)

### 5. *The Mexican Boundary Survey: A History*
**Author:** Paula Rebert
**Publisher:** University of Texas Press (2001)
**Why:** Detailed account of the actual boundary survey work, including the Disturnell map errors and their consequences.
**Price:** ~$35
**ISBN:** 978-0292771116

### 6. *Defiant Peacemaker: Nicholas Trist in the Mexican War*
**Author:** Wallace Ohrt
**Publisher:** Texas A&M University Press (1997)
**Why:** Biography of the US negotiator who drafted the treaty. Recommended by Library of Congress.
**Price:** ~$30
**ISBN:** 978-0890967935

## Priority 3: Maps & Primary Sources

### 7. Disturnell Map (1847) - High Resolution
**Source:** Library of Congress or US Capitol Visitor Center
**URL:** https://www.visitthecapitol.gov/artifact/mapa-de-los-estados-unidos-de-mejico-map-united-states-mexico-j-disturnell-1847
**Why:** THE map referenced in the treaty with the errors. Essential visual.
**Cost:** Free (public domain)

### 8. Treaty of Guadalupe Hidalgo - Original Document Images
**Source:** National Archives
**URL:** https://www.archives.gov/milestone-documents/treaty-of-guadalupe-hidalgo
**Why:** Show actual treaty pages on screen.
**Cost:** Free

### 9. Gadsden Purchase Treaty - Full Text
**Source:** Yale Avalon Project / National Archives
**URL:** https://avalon.law.yale.edu/19th_century/mx1853.asp
**Why:** The "patch" treaty that fixed the border.
**Cost:** Free

## Priority 4: Context & Mexican Perspective

### 10. *The United States and Mexico at War: Nineteenth-Century Expansionism and Conflict*
**Editor:** Donald S. Frazier
**Publisher:** Macmillan Reference (1998)
**Why:** Encyclopedia covering all aspects of the war and its aftermath.
**Price:** ~$150 (reference volume, check library)
**ISBN:** 978-0028646060

### 11. *Santa Anna of Mexico*
**Author:** Will Fowler
**Publisher:** University of Nebraska Press (2007)
**Why:** Academic biography of Santa Anna, including his motivations for the Gadsden Purchase.
**Price:** ~$30
**ISBN:** 978-0803260139
