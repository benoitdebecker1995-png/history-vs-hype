## NOTES FOR EDITOR

**Map Usage:**
- Maps should be on screen 3-5 seconds minimum (viewers need time to comprehend)
- Use zoom/pan on detailed maps to show specific areas mentioned
- Color-code consistently (e.g., always same color for Green Line)

**Document Usage:**
- Show title/header for 1-2 seconds
- Zoom to relevant passage for 2-3 seconds
- Don't need full document, just enough to verify it exists

**Photo Usage:**
- Settlement photos: 2-3 seconds each
- News headlines: 1-2 seconds (can be faster)
- Political figures: 2 seconds while mentioned

**Pacing:**
- Historical section (1916-1995): Heavy B-roll, fast cuts
- Modern section (2024-2025): More talking head, selective B-roll for impact
- Total B-roll time: ~3-4 minutes of 10-minute video
