**2. UN General Assembly Resolution 181 (II) - Partition Plan (1947)**

**3. UN Security Council Resolution 242 (1967)**
- **Source:** UN Website

- **Key questions:** What exactly did it demand? Ambiguous wording about "territories" vs "the territories"

**4. Oslo II Accord (1995) - Israeli-Palestinian Interim Agreement**
- **Source:** UN Website
- **URL:** https://www.un.org/unispal/document/auto-insert-185434/
- **Format:** PDF (long document, ~100 pages)

- **Key questions:** What were the exact percentages? What was the intended duration? What was supposed to happen?

**5. ICJ Advisory Opinion on Legal Consequences of Israeli Occupation (2024)**
- **Source:** International Court of Justice
- **URL:** https://www.icj-cij.org/ (search for 2024 Palestine opinion)

- **Key questions:** What exactly did ICJ rule illegal? What specific remedies ordered? What evidence cited?

**6. UN General Assembly Resolution on Palestine (September 2025)**

- **Why:** Verify vote count (142-10-12), exact demands, 12-month timeline
- **Key questions:** What was the exact vote? What did resolution demand? What timeline given?
