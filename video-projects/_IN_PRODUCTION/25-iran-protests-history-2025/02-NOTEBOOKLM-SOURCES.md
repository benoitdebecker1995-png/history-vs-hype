## TIER 1: Must-Have Academic Sources

### [A1] Abrahamian, Ervand - *A History of Modern Iran*
- **Publisher:** Cambridge University Press, 2008
- **ISBN:** 978-0521528917
- **Price:** ~$35-40
- **Why:** Standard academic reference. Covers 1900-2008 comprehensively.
- **Key Chapters:** Ch. 1-3 (Constitutional Revolution), Ch. 4-5 (Pahlavi era), Ch. 6-8 (Islamic Republic)
- **Status:** ⏳ PURCHASE

### [A2] Abrahamian, Ervand - *Khomeinism: Essays on the Islamic Republic*
- **Publisher:** University of California Press, 1993
- **ISBN:** 978-0520085039
- **Price:** ~$30
- **Why:** Deep analysis of Islamic Republic's ideology and mechanisms
- **Key Chapters:** All essays relevant
- **Status:** ⏳ PURCHASE

### [A3] Parsa, Misagh - *Democracy in Iran: Why It Failed and How It Might Succeed*
- **Publisher:** Harvard University Press, 2016
- **ISBN:** 978-0674545137
- **Price:** ~$45
- **Why:** Comparative analysis of ALL protest movements (1999, 2009, 2017)
- **Key Chapters:** Entire book focuses on thesis question
- **Status:** ⏳ PURCHASE (HIGH PRIORITY)

### [A4] Ansari, Ali - *Modern Iran Since 1797: Reform and Revolution*
- **Publisher:** Routledge, 3rd edition 2019
- **ISBN:** 978-1138281677
- **Price:** ~$50
- **Why:** Long historical arc from Qajar dynasty to present
- **Status:** ⏳ PURCHASE

## TIER 3: Primary Source Collections

### [P1] 1906 Iranian Constitution
- **Source:** Multiple academic translations available
- **Access:** Wikisource (Persian), academic editions
- **Why:** Original constitutional demands—show on screen
- **Status:** ✅ FREE ONLINE

### [P2] 1979 Constitution of Islamic Republic
- **Source:** Official English translation + academic commentary
- **Access:** Available online
- **Key Articles:** Article 5 (Velayat-e Faqih), Article 110 (Supreme Leader powers), Article 91-99 (Guardian Council)
- **Status:** ✅ FREE ONLINE

### [P3] FRUS (Foreign Relations of the United States) - Iran Volumes
- **Source:** US State Department
- **Access:** Free online at history.state.gov
- **Relevant Volumes:**
  - Volume X (Iran 1951-1954) - Mossadegh/Coup
  - Iran volumes 1969-1976
  - Iran: Revolution 1977-1980
- **Status:** ✅ FREE ONLINE

### [P4] Declassified CIA Documents - Operation TPAJAX
- **Source:** National Security Archive, George Washington University
- **Access:** Free online
- **Why:** Show actual planning documents for 1953 coup
- **Status:** ✅ FREE ONLINE
