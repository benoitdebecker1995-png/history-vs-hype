## 1905-1911: CONSTITUTIONAL REVOLUTION

### Key Facts (Tier 4 - Verify with Academic Sources)

❓ **"Iran's first revolution in 1906 provided the country with a constitution and parliament"** - Gresham College

❓ **"The Constitutional Revolution in Persia was the first mass movement of the 20th century in the Islamic world"** - Academic source

### Timeline
- **December 1905:** Protests against flogging of merchants for sugar prices
- **January 12, 1906:** Shah agrees to "house of justice"
- **August 5, 1906:** Royal decree establishing Majles (parliament)
- **October 1906:** First Majles opens
- **December 30, 1906:** Fundamental Laws passed

### Demands
1. ❓ Constitution limiting royal power
2. ❓ Parliament (Majles) with legislative authority
3. ❓ "House of justice" (courts)
4. ❓ National bank owned by nation

### Composition of First Majles
- 60+ bazaaris (merchants)
- 25 clerics
- 50 landlords and notables

**Key parallel to 2025:** Merchants (bazaaris) led both 1905 and 2025 protests.
