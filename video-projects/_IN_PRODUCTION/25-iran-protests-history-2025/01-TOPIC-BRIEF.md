## Differentiation Strategy: Mechanism Forensics

**Core insight:** Iranians aren't just protesting "repression"—they're protesting a SPECIFIC SYSTEM with identifiable mechanisms.

### The Mechanism Forensics Approach

Instead of: "The Islamic Republic is authoritarian"

Show the EXACT MECHANISMS:

**1. Guardian Council Veto System**
- 12 members (6 clerics appointed by Supreme Leader, 6 jurists)
- Can disqualify ANY candidate from ANY election
- 2021 election: Disqualified 98% of presidential candidates
- **On screen:** Show the actual constitutional article + disqualification lists

**2. IRGC Economic Control**
- Controls estimated 20-40% of Iranian economy
- Construction, telecom, oil
- Creates vested interest in regime survival
- **On screen:** Show corporate structure diagrams, ownership data

**3. Basij Mobilization Structure**
- 5-20 million members (estimates vary)
- Present in every neighborhood, school, workplace
- Rewards: University admissions, jobs, social status
- **On screen:** Show recruitment documents, benefit structures

**4. Friday Prayer Network**
- Supreme Leader appoints prayer leaders in every city
- Weekly political messaging to millions
- Direct line from Supreme Leader to population
- **On screen:** Show appointment documents, sermon transcripts

## Historical Arc: The Protest Cycle

**Thesis:** Iran has been in a century-long cycle of constitutionalism vs. authoritarianism.

### Timeline Structure

| Year | Event | Pattern |
|------|-------|---------|
| 1905-1911 | Constitutional Revolution | DEMAND: Constitution, parliament, limits on power |
| 1953 | Mossadegh Overthrow | CRUSHING: External intervention, authoritarian restoration |
| 1963 | White Revolution Protests | SUPPRESSION: Shah crushes clerical opposition |
| 1979 | Islamic Revolution | NEW CYCLE: Promise of Islamic democracy |
| 1999 | Student Protests | SUPPRESSION: First post-revolution protests crushed |
| 2009 | Green Movement | CRUSHING: Millions protest, regime survives |
| 2017-2019 | Economic Protests | SUPPRESSION: Hundreds killed |
| 2022 | Woman Life Freedom | ONGOING: Same pattern repeating |

**Key insight to prove:** Each generation rediscovers the same demand—constitutional limits on power—and faces the same suppression mechanisms.
