## PRIMARY SOURCES (SMOKING GUNS)

### 1. The 2009 Self-Rule Act - Section 21
> "If the people of Greenland take a decision in favour of independence, negotiations are to commence between the Danish Government and Naalakkersuisut regarding the introduction of independence for Greenland."

**Why it matters:** This is the law Trump doesn't understand. Greenland can't be bought because Greenlanders have the legal right to decide their own future.

**Source:** [Danish Prime Minister's Office (English text)](https://english.stm.dk/media/4vgewyoh/gl-selvstyrelov-uk.pdf)

### 2. The 2009 Act Preamble - International Law Recognition
> "The people of Greenland is a people pursuant to international law with the right of self-determination."

**Why it matters:** Denmark officially recognized Greenlanders as a distinct people with self-determination rights. This isn't colonial territory - it's a people with internationally recognized rights.

### 3. 1953 Thule Relocation - Danish Supreme Court Ruling
> "The forced movement was decided and carried out in such a way, and under such circumstances, that it has to be regarded as a serious encroachment towards the people."

**Source:** Danish High Court (2003), European Court of Human Rights case HINGITAQ 53 v. Denmark

### 4. 1999 Prime Minister Apology
> On 2 September 1999, the Danish Prime Minister formally apologised to the applicants for the forced relocation of the Inughuit in 1953.

### 5. 2025 Election Results - Demokraatik Leader Statement
> "We don't want to be Americans. No, we don't want to be Danes. We want to be Greenlanders. And we want our own independence in the future."
- Nielsen, leader of winning Democrats party, March 2025

### 6. Trump's Davos Reversal (January 21, 2026)
Trump reversed his position at the 2026 Davos conference, pledging not to use military force against Greenland or to tariff any European countries.
