## FACT-CHECK REQUIREMENTS

**Target:** 90%+ verification before filming

**Every claim must have:**
1. Source citation (court ruling, UN document, etc.)
2. Specific paragraph or page number
3. Direct quote where possible
4. Verification status (✅ or ⚠️)

**Red Lines:**
❌ NEVER say "ICJ ruled Gaza is genocide" (no final ruling yet)
❌ NEVER take political sides on pending cases
❌ NEVER cite death tolls without source
❌ NEVER present opinion as legal fact

**Green Lights:**
✅ "The ICJ issued provisional measures but hasn't ruled on merits"
✅ "Legal scholars are divided on whether..."
✅ "Courts found genocide in Rwanda and Bosnia but..."
✅ Show exact Convention text on screen
