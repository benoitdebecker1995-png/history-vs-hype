## 💡 PRO TIPS

### **Title Writing:**
1. **Name drop when possible** - "JD Vance" > "A politician"
2. **Be specific** - "Vatican Archives" > "church documents"
3. **Use active voice** - "I checked" > "An investigation was conducted"
4. **Front-load keywords** - "JD Vance" at start > end
5. **Test on mobile** - Looks different than desktop

### **Description Writing:**
1. **First 150 characters matter most** - That's what shows before "Show More"
2. **Link your own videos** - Keeps viewers on your channel
3. **Update old videos** - Add links to new related videos
4. **Use line breaks** - Wall of text = nobody reads
5. **Include searchable phrases** - "Did Christianity invent human rights" = search query

### **Common Mistakes:**
- ❌ No timestamps (viewers leave to find them elsewhere)
- ❌ No source links (skeptics assume you're making it up)
- ❌ Generic hashtags only (#history #video #youtube = worthless)
- ❌ No CTA (missed engagement opportunity)
- ❌ Copying competitor descriptions word-for-word (algorithm can tell)
