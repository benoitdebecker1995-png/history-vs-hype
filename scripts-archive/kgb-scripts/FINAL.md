## PRODUCTION NOTES:

**Key Visual Elements:**
- High-contrast graphics emphasizing documentary evidence over speculation
- Maps showing operational networks without excessive detail
- Clear source attribution graphics for major claims
- Timeline visuals connecting documented operations to historical context
- Balanced analysis charts showing both KGB success and limitations

**Audio Emphasis:**
- Source attribution woven naturally into narration
- Clear distinction between documented facts and analytical interpretation
- Smooth transitions between revealing operations and acknowledging limits
- Authority-building language emphasizing archival evidence

**Fact-Check Priority:**
- Every major claim attributed to specific documented sources
- Clear separation between operational capabilities and political outcomes
- Acknowledgment of evidence limitations while maintaining analytical rigor
- Balanced presentation that neither minimizes KGB operations nor ignores Palestinian agency

**Tone Calibration:**
- Dramatic revelation of documented operations
- Measured analysis of complex political dynamics  
- Authority-focused conclusion emphasizing evidence-based approach
- Timeless messaging about the importance of documentary truth over propaganda

**Runtime Management:**
- Segment 1: ~75 seconds (hook and setup)
- Segment 2: ~165 seconds (operations focus)
- Segment 3: ~150 seconds (narrative focus) 
- Segment 4: ~105 seconds (limitations)
- Segment 5: ~105 seconds (conclusion)
- Total: ~10 minutes of narration
