### MAIN BODY

#### MYTH 1: "France Brought Civilization/Development" (2:30 - 4:30)
**The Operation Persil Smoking Gun**

- **Myth presentation** (30 seconds): Common belief that French presence helped develop former colonies
- **Counter-evidence reveal** (90 seconds):
  - **1958 context**: Guinea votes "Non" to French partnership under Sékou Touré
  - **French retaliation**: Complete infrastructure withdrawal
  - **Operation Persil revelation**: "French intelligence used government printing presses to create false Guinean banknotes, which were then poured en masse into the country"
  - **Result**: "The collapse of the Guinean economy... never fully recovered"
- **Source credibility**: Fanny Pigeaud and Ndongo Samba Sylla, expert economists on French monetary control
- **Impact analysis** (30 seconds): This proves French policy was designed for punishment, not prosperity

**Project Notes:**
- *This is your smoking gun evidence—let it breathe*
- *Visual: Show real vs. counterfeit currency side-by-side*
- *Tone: Let the facts speak—this evidence is so damning it needs minimal commentary*
- *Bridge line: "If this is how France treats countries that choose independence, what about their military interventions?"*

#### MYTH 2: "French Military Intervention Prevents Chaos" (4:30 - 6:30)
**Strategic Failure Documentation**

- **Myth presentation** (30 seconds): French claim that Operation Serval/Barkhane brought stability to Mali
- **Counter-evidence barrage** (90 seconds):
  - **Mission scope**: 2013 launch with 5,100 troops, fighter jets, drones—overwhelming force advantage
  - **Documented failure**: "Attacks by armed groups increased substantially, leading to tens of thousands of fatalities"
  - **Regional spread**: "Militants spread to neighboring countries, now threaten West African coastal states"
  - **Political collapse**: "Mali's military launched two coups and supplanted the democratic government"
- **Source authority**: Eloïse Bertrand, Tony Chafer & Ed Stoddard (UK security and strategy experts)
- **Irony emphasis** (30 seconds): France had every advantage but achieved the opposite of stated goals
