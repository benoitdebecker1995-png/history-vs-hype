## OBJECTIVE 4: QANON CONNECTION DATA

### The Statistic - VERIFIED

**Study Found:** Weinberg et al. (2025) analyzing **1.26 million QAnon posts** found **34.79% of users engaged in antisemitic content**

- **Title:** "Hidden in plain sight: antisemitic content in QAnon subreddits"
- **Authors:** Dana B. Weinberg (lead, Queens College-CUNY), Meyer D. Levy, April Edwards, Jeffrey S. Kopstein, David Frey, Peter Antonaros, Nikola Baci, Noah D. Cohen, Javier A. Fernandez, Yunis Ni
- **Publication:** PLOS ONE
- **Published:** March 19, 2025

- **Data Source:** Two QAnon subreddits (r/CBTS_Stream and r/greatawakening)

- **Users:** 34,500 total users
- **Method:** Content network analysis and qualitative coding using expert-generated lists of explicit and implicit antisemitic terms

**Key Findings Breakdown:**
- **6.84%** posted EXPLICIT antisemitic content
- **27.95%** posted IMPLICIT antisemitic content only
- **34.79% TOTAL** engaged in antisemitic content (combined explicit + implicit)

**Four Dimensions of Antisemitism:**
1. Hidden Jewish Power (56% of implicit terms)
2. Distasteful Jewish Traits/Blood Libel (57% of implicit terms)

## OBJECTIVE 5: COUNTER-NARRATIVE PREPARATION

### Objection 1: "But what if there's some truth to it?" / "It predicted real events"

**The Objection:** Defenders claim that even if the Protocols is a forgery, it "predicted" real events like the Russian Revolution, world wars, economic upheavals, or contemporary geopolitics.

**Rebuttals:**

**1. Confirmation Bias Phenomenon**
- **Source:** Scientific American / Psychology research
- **Key Point:** "Confirmation bias" is the most pervasive cognitive bias driving belief in conspiracies. People give more weight to evidence supporting existing beliefs and ignore contradictory evidence
- **Application:** The Protocols contains vague, apocalyptic predictions that can be retrofitted to almost any major historical event through selective interpretation

**2. Historian Stephen Whitfield (Brandeis University)**
- **Source:** https://www.brandeis.edu/now/2020/september/whitfield-conversation.html
- **Quote:** "The Protocols thus have no importance in themselves; they are spurious. But they do bestow precision upon apocalyptic fears, which could not survive without some ingredient of plausibility – however wildly far-fetched."
- **Explanation:** The text exploits real historical anxieties (economic instability, social change) and presents vague predictions that believers can interpret to "fit" actual events. This is a classic propaganda technique, not genuine prophecy.

**3. The Updating Problem**
- **Source:** Randall Bytwerk, Calvin University
- **Key Point:** The vague nature of the Protocols' "predictions" allows it to be constantly adapted to current events in any era
- **Evidence:** Different editions in different countries modified the text to address local concerns, proving it's not genuine prophecy but flexible propaganda
- **Example:** Different editions have been updated to "predict" the Cold War, 9/11, and COVID-19

**Video Framing:** "When people say the Protocols 'predicted' events, they're experiencing what psychologists call confirmation bias—seeing patterns that aren't there. The text is deliberately vague, like a fortune teller's prediction, so it can be retrofitted to any major event. Real historical documents don't need constant updating to 'stay relevant'—but the Protocols has been rewritten dozens of times to address everything from the Cold War to 9/11 to COVID-19. That's propaganda, not prophecy."

**The Objection:** Hitler and Nazi leaders promoted the Protocols, with Goebbels saying he believed in its "inner truth" even if not its factual truth. Doesn't this suggest something valid?

**2. What "Inner Truth" Actually Means**
- **Source:** USHMM Holocaust Encyclopedia

**Key Quote:**
"Joseph Goebbels, Nazi Germany's Minister of Public Enlightenment and Propaganda, understood that the Protocols could be used to demonize Jews. Long before he assumed the role, Goebbels referenced the Protocols in his diary. He wrote: 'I believe that The Protocols of the Elders of Zion is a forgery.' However, Goebbels continued, **'I believe in the inner, but not the factual, truth of The Protocols.' **3. Nazi Leaders KNEW It Was Fake**

- **Source:** Stephen Whitfield (Brandeis): "Hitler himself was unsure of the authenticity of the Protocols—a question of verification that may not have mattered all that much to the Nazis. **4. Historian Analysis**
- **Source:** Randall Bytwerk, "Believing in 'Inner Truth': The Protocols in Nazi Propaganda, 1933-1945"

**Key Findings:**
